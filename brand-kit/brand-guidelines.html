01 — Overview

Brand Overview & Mission

AIPowerStacks exists to bring clarity to the exploding AI tools landscape. As AI subscriptions multiply, costs compound silently. We help users take control — discovering what's available, comparing alternatives, tracking what they pay, and eliminating waste.

Our brand is built on precision, efficiency, and trust. The visual identity reflects stacking and organizing — the core metaphor of taming a chaotic tool stack.

Core Values

📊
Clarity

Cut through noise. Give users exactly what they need to make smart decisions.

⚡
Efficiency

Save time and money. Every feature should reduce waste, not add to it.

🔍
Transparency

No hidden agendas. Open pricing data. Honest comparisons.

02 — Logo

Logo Usage

The AIPowerStacks logo consists of a stacked bars icon paired with the wordmark. The icon represents the product concept — tools stacked and organized, with three bars of decreasing width symbolizing hierarchy and filtering.

Logo Variants

Full White — use on dark backgrounds
AIPowerStacks
Full Dark — use on light backgrounds where red doesn't work
Icon Only — for favicons, app icons, profile images

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Always maintain a clear space of at least ½× the icon height on all sides. Minimum icon size: 24px. Minimum full logo width: 180px.

Logo Don'ts

  • Stretch or distort the logo proportions
  • Use the logo on backgrounds that create insufficient contrast
  • Rotate or flip the logo or icon
  • Change the icon's bar proportions or spacing
  • Apply drop shadows, glows, or filters to the wordmark
  • Use the icon without the red background in a colored context
  • Recreate the logo in a different font or style
  • Add taglines directly attached to the wordmark

05 — Voice & Tone

Voice & Tone

AIPowerStacks speaks like a sharp, helpful colleague — not a corporate press release or a hype machine. We're direct, practical, and a little opinionated.

✓ We Are

  • Direct and confident
  • Practical and action-oriented
  • Helpful without being patronizing
  • Honest about trade-offs and costs
  • Slightly irreverent (we mock subscription bloat)
  • Precise with numbers and data

✗ We're Not

  • Hyped or buzzwordy
  • Passive or wishy-washy
  • Preachy or moralizing
  • Overly formal or corporate
  • Sycophantic ("Great choice!")
  • Vague ("powerful AI solutions")

Example Copy

✓ GOOD

"You're paying for 3 tools that all do the same thing. Here's the cheapest one."

✗ AVOID

"Leverage our powerful AI-driven platform to synergize your software procurement journey."
