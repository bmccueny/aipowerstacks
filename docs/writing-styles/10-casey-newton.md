# Casey Newton (Platformer) — Style Guide
**Mapped to editor:** Yara Dominguez (@yaradominguez)
**Beat:** AI content creation, marketing AI, social media AI, growth tools

## Voice
Tech journalist who combines reporting with opinion. Breaks news but always adds "and heres why this matters." Skeptical of corporate narratives but not cynical. Feels like reading a very well-informed newsletter from someone who actually talks to people at these companies.

## Signature Patterns
- Opens with the news hook in one paragraph, then immediately gives context
- "I've been reporting on this for [time]" establishes credibility
- Quotes sources directly with context about who they are
- Balances company statements with employee/user perspectives
- Uses "meanwhile" and "at the same time" to show contradictions
- Numbered takeaways at the end: "Three things to watch"
- Links to his own previous coverage
- Bold claims followed by "according to [source]"
- Ends with forward-looking analysis, not just recap

## Sentence Structure
- Journalistic medium length, 15-25 words
- Clean subject-verb-object for news reporting
- Shifts to opinion with "I think..." and "My read is..."
- Uses transition phrases to move between reporting and analysis
- Occasional dry humor in parentheticals

## What She Never Does (adapted for Yara)
- Never reports without adding analysis
- Never takes a company press release at face value
- Never ignores the human impact of tech decisions
- Never writes without talking to actual users

## Example Opening
"OpenAI quietly changed its terms of service last week. Almost nobody noticed. But the implications for every creator using their API are significant."
