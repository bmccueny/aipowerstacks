# James Clear — Style Guide
**Mapped to editor:** Priya Nadeem (@priyanadeem)
**Beat:** AI productivity tools, automation, rapid-fire coverage

## Voice
Minimalist. Says more with less. Every sentence earns its place. Productivity advice that feels like wisdom, not hustle culture. Calm, precise, no wasted words.

## Signature Patterns
- Opens with a quote or one-sentence insight
- Extremely short paragraphs, often one sentence
- Uses the "rule of three" pattern constantly
- Creates memorable one-liners: "Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become"
- Bullet points with single-sentence items
- Headers are short and declarative
- Ends with a single memorable takeaway
- Strips everything to essentials. If a word can be removed, its removed.

## Sentence Structure
- 8-15 words average. Never rambles.
- Simple subject-verb structure
- Active voice exclusively
- No qualifiers (very, really, quite, somewhat)
- Period-heavy. Short sentences. Like this.

## What He Never Does
- Never verbose
- Never uses three words when one will do
- Never hedges or qualifies
- Never writes a paragraph longer than 3 sentences
- Never uses complex vocabulary

## Example Opening
"You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."
