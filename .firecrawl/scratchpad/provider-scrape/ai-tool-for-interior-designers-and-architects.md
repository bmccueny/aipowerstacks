## Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ButterflAI and how it can scale your ecommerce growth

What is ButterflAI?

ButterflAI is an AI-powered platform for ecommerce teams that automates catalog optimization at scale. Using advanced AI Agents, it generates and improves product titles, descriptions, SEO metadata, images, and videos in bulk, allowing you to manage thousands of SKUs with consistency and keyword alignment.

Why use ButterflAI instead of ChatGPT?

Unlike generic AI like ChatGPT, ButterflAI is purpose-built for ecommerce. It uses your specific store data, brand guidelines, and market trends to generate conversion-focused content in bulk. Plus, it integrates directly with your CMS to publish updates instantly.

Does it integrate with my ecommerce platform?

Yes. ButterflAI has native integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, PrestaShop, and LogiCommerce. This allows you to sync your products and publish AI-optimized content with a single click. For other platforms, you can use our CSV/Excel import tools or our flexible API.

Does using AI content harm my SEO?

No, Google and AI engines actually reward high-quality, relevant content. ButterflAI is designed to create helpful, keyword-aligned descriptions and metadata that improve both traditional SEO and visibility in AI search results (GEO).

Can I generate product videos with ButterflAI?

Absolutely. Our AI Video Agent transforms static product photos into engaging short-form videos (reels/tiktoks) automatically, helping you maintain a consistent social media presence without manual editing.

How do credits work?

ButterflAI uses a flexible credit system. Credits are consumed when you generate or optimize content (images, videos, or copy). Each plan includes a monthly allowance that you can use across any of our tools based on your specific needs.

How do I get started?

Request a demo to see ButterflAI in action with your own products. We'll show you how to set up your brand voice and start optimizing your catalog in minutes.
