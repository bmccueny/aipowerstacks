# About

Boring Report is an app that removes sensationalism from the news and makes it boring to read. Catchy headlines often distract readers from actual facts and relevant information.

Using advanced AI language models, Boring Report processes exciting articles and transforms them into the content you see. This helps readers focus on essential details and minimizes sensationalism.
