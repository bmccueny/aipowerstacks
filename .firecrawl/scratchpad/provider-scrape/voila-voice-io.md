## Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TiLT, and what can it do for me?

TiLT is an AI-powered teaching and learning platform that transforms your static content into dynamic multimedia experiences. Whether you upload a PDF, paste a URL, TiLT turns it into localised videos, quizzes, and more. Just sign up, upload your content, and let TiLT handle the rest.

What formats does TiLT support, and can I change or upgrade my plan easily?

You can upload PDFs, PPTs, DOCs, and even paste links (like Wikipedia or web articles). TiLT supports easy plan upgrades and content migration, so you can scale without friction.

What is  AL, and how do I interact with the AI Tutor?

AL is TiLT’s AI-powered learning companion. Students can ask questions, revisit concepts, or get personalised support in their preferred language — all through voice or text.

Can I convert documents into editable presentations or videos?

Yes! Effortlessly convert or translate documents, notes, or PDFs into editable PPTX files and interactive videos tailored to diverse learning styles.

Can AI help me teach in multiple languages?

Yes! TiLT offers one-click translation with voice localization for over 30+ languages — ideal for global learners.

How can I make my online lessons more engaging for students?

Use TiLT to enhance your content with avatars, dynamic visuals, and quizzes — all auto-generated from your uploads.

What is an AI learning assistant, and how does it work?

AL (TiLT's assistant) helps learners by summarising content, answering questions, and adapting to their pace.

Is there an easy way to turn my teaching deck or presentation into videos?

Definitely. Transform your pre-made slides into dynamic, learner-friendly videos with just a few clicks.
