### What is a Telegram CRM?

A Telegram CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a software solution that helps businesses manage their Telegram contacts, automate messaging, track sales pipelines, and support customers directly within the Telegram ecosystem.

### How does the Telegram CRM integration work?

Our Telegram CRM integration connects your Telegram accounts and bots to a unified dashboard. It syncs chat history, user profiles, and group activities in real-time, allowing your team to collaborate seamlessly without sharing physical devices.

### Can I use a CRM Telegram bot for customer support?

Yes! A CRM Telegram bot is perfect for automating first-line customer support. It can answer common questions, collect user information, and automatically route complex issues to the right human agent in your TGDesk dashboard.

### Is TGDesk suitable as a Telegram CRM for Web3?

Absolutely. TGDesk is highly optimized as a Web3 CRM for Telegram. It helps crypto projects manage large communities, track multi-account outreach, and securely handle investor relations through advanced group management tools.

### How does the Telegram lead generation help?

Our Telegram lead generation tools allow you to extract and organize contact info from incoming chats and group interactions. You can tag users, set follow-up reminders, and push them through your sales funnel, turning casual chatters into paying customers.

### Why choose a native Telegram CRM?

Traditional CRMs treat Telegram as an afterthought. A native Telegram CRM like TGDesk is built specifically for the Telegram ecosystem. It natively supports multi-account management, deep group tracking, and built-in Telegram sender features that generic CRMs simply cannot offer.
