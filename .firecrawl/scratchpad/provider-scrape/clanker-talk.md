FAQ

## Questions we get asked  (so you don't have to call us).

### How does Clanker Talk work?

You describe what you need asked, pick a business or enter any phone number, and our AI voice agent makes the call. It handles the conversation, extracts key information, and delivers structured results back to you in real time.

### What kind of calls can it make?

Anything you'd normally dread calling about — wait times, store hours, appointment availability, prices, reservation openings, office admin, and personal calls too. If it involves a phone and a question, we've got it.

### How much does it cost?

You get 1 free call when you sign up — no credit card required. After that, credit packs start at $5 for 5 calls ($1/call) down to $0.60/call for the 50-pack. Credits never expire. No subscriptions.

### What if a call fails?

If a call doesn't connect or fails before the conversation starts, your credit is automatically refunded. No charge, no mystery.

### Can I watch the call happen?

Yep. The live timeline shows every status change and transcript line as the conversation unfolds. Then you get a tidy structured summary when it's done.

### Does the business know it's AI?

The agent is designed to be conversational and natural. It identifies itself appropriately and handles calls with the same courtesy a human would — just without the nervous energy.
