### [Frequently Asked Questions](https://mexty.ai/ressources/faq)

Everything you need to know about getting started with Mexty.

What is Mexty?

Mexty is a no-code, AI-powered authoring platform that enables educators, trainers, instructional designers, and parents to create interactive, SCORM-compliant e-learning content in minutes—no coding or technical skills required.

How does Mexty work?

Just type a prompt—like "Create an interactive quiz on the Solar System for 6th grade"—and Mexty's AI course creation engine instantly generates lessons, quizzes, or activities. You can then refine them using our intuitive drag-and-drop LMS content editor. [See our getting started guide](https://mexty.ai/ressources/getting-started)

Do I need design or coding skills to use Mexty?

No. Mexty is completely no-code with zero learning curve. If you can describe your training or lesson, Mexty can create it.

Can Mexty personalize content for different learners?

Yes. Mexty adapts learning content to different learning styles, skill levels, and paces, and supports multimedia, branching scenarios, and gamified e-learning activities to boost engagement. [Request a demo](https://mexty.ai/contact)

Who can use Mexty?

Ideal for K–12 teachers, higher education faculty, corporate trainers, and instructional designers, Mexty is also perfect for parents creating personalized learning activities at home.

What types of content can I create?

From full e-learning courses and microlearning modules to interactive quizzes, training simulations, and gamified exercises, Mexty supports a wide range of formats for education and training.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. You can try Mexty for free to explore its AI-powered course creation features and see how easily you can create SCORM-compliant interactive learning content for any LMS. [Try Mexty for free](https://mexty.ai/pricing)
