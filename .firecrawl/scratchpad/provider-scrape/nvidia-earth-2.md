## Overview


Making Weather AI Accessible Worldwide



NVIDIA Earth-2 is a comprehensive family of open models, libraries, and frameworks that democratize global access to professional-grade weather and climate AI. By integrating state-of-the-art architectures, the platform provides a fully open software stack that accelerates every stage of forecasting, from data processing to high-resolution visualization. This shift from resource-intensive physics models to an end-to-end AI pipeline lets researchers, startups, and government agencies run, fine-tune, and deploy sophisticated forecasting systems on their own infrastructure with unprecedented speed and cost-efficiency.

Models and Frameworks



## The Earth-2 Model Family

NVIDIA Earth-2 makes weather AI accessible worldwide at every stage, from processing observation data for initial states to generating local storm predictions and 15-day global forecasts.

![Atlas architecture 15-day weather forecasts in advance](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

### Earth-2 Medium Range

The Atlas architecture delivers high-accuracy predictions for over 70 weather variables up to 15 days in advance, outperforming leading open models like GenCast on key industry benchmarks.

![Earth-2 StormScope AI weather nowcasting](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

### Earth-2 Nowcasting

Using generative AI to predict satellite and radar imagery, Earth-2 Nowcasting delivers high-fidelity, zero- to six-hour hazardous weather forecasts in minutes. It’s the first AI model to outperform traditional physics-based systems in simulating storm dynamics.

![Earth-2 HealDA AI data assimilation](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

### Earth-2 Global Data Assimilation (Coming Soon)

The Earth-2 Global Data Assimilations model generates precise initial atmospheric conditions in seconds on GPUs rather than hours on supercomputers. When coupled with Earth-2 Medium Range, it delivers the most skillful forecasting predictions produced by an open, entirely AI pipeline.

![Earth-2 CorrDiff AI downscaling](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

### Earth-2 CorrDiff

CorrDiff enables generative AI downscaling 500x faster with a 10,000x improvement in energy efficiency. This augments current applications and workflows, allowing enterprises to generate more datasets for better probabilistic distributions of weather events​.​

![Earth-2 FourCastNet3 AI forecasting](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

### Earth-2 FourCastNet 3

FourCastNet 3 accelerates AI-based global weather forecasting. With it, enterprises can develop solutions using datasets that are up to 20x larger, capture extreme weather events while maintaining or boosting energy efficiency.
