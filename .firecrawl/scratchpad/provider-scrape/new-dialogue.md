### Questions, answered with clarity.

Following are general frequently asked questions from our users. Please [contact us](https://www.newdialogue.com/company/contact-us) if you have further questions.

### What does New Dialogue AI do?

Imagine the power of having ChatGPT inside your organization, but where it knows all about your organization (that you give it permission to have access to). Your content (files you create), your data sitting inside other systems (HR, CRM, etc.), and then imagine being able to simply automate time consuming tasks, using agents that you create yourself. New Dialogue AI and your knowledge give you an oracle on your operation.

### Do you train your models on our proprietary information?

No. Only if you ask us to. For general production deployments, New Dialogue AI simply uses RAG for generative output. We can fine tune a model with your information should that be what you want to achieve.

### What is enterprise pricing?

The pricing listed on our website is for our SaaS offering. There’s no functional difference between New Dialogue AI SaaS and our Enterprise offering, other than how we deploy New Dialogue AI to your cloud tenancy. [Contact us](https://www.newdialogue.com/company/contact-us) for more information and pricing for your organization.

### Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Scroll to the top and tap or click on Sign Up. You can create a free 7-day trial and have access to the whole platform.

### Do you keep any information I upload during the trial?

At the end of 7 days we’ll permanently delete your account and all associated content.
