# Film Flow

## AI powered tool for visualizing and analyzing the emotional pulse of films. Harnessing the power of AI, Film Flow allows you to delve deep into the captivating world of cinema and gain valuable insights into the emotional dynamics of any movie.

Visualize

When you enter your API Key in this app, your browser establishes a direct connection with OpenAI. We do not store or utilize your key in any way.
