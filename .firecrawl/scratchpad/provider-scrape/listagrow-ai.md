## Frequently asked questions

What is Listagrow AI, and how does it work?
+

Listagrow is an AI-powered product listing suite for e-commerce businesses, agencies, and online sellers. It analyses your raw product images and applies your brand guidelines to generate complete, ready-to-publish listings - title, description, SEO metadata, product tags, category, image alt text, and custom added fields. You can generate new products in bulk from images, enrich an existing catalog, and enhance product photos with AI-generated professional imagery. All generated content lands in a review queue where you can approve, edit, or regenerate individual fields before exporting to your platform.


What exactly does Listagrow generate for each product?
+

For every product, Listagrow generates a complete listing package: a compelling **product title**, a persuasive **product description** (with your chosen structure and tone), a full set of **product tags** for internal search, a **product category**, an SEO **meta title and meta description**, **image alt text** for every uploaded photo, and suggestions for variant options. All of this is generated simultaneously from your product images and brand guidelines.


Do I need a Shopify store to use Listagrow SaaS?
+

No. The Listagrow SaaS platform at **app.listagrow.com** works independently of any e-commerce platform. You can generate, enhance, and export product listings whether you sell on Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Amazon, Etsy, your own website, or any combination of channels. You simply export a formatted CSV for whichever platform you use. (We also offer a dedicated [Shopify App](https://apps.shopify.com/ai-product-content-generator) with native integration if you prefer working directly inside your Shopify admin.)


How does the Brand & Content Guidelines feature work?
+

In your Brand Settings you define: your **tone of voice** (professional, friendly, luxurious, etc.), your **target customer** profile, your preferred **language**, your **brand keywords and selling points** that should always appear, your preferred **description structure** (features, benefits, use cases, specifications, etc.), and formatting rules like title length, bullet points, and heading style. Every listing generated thereafter follows these rules automatically - no prompting required.


What is an AI credit and how many do I need?
+

Credits are the usage currency. Generating a full product listing (all fields) costs **1 credit per 5 images** in the group. Regenerating a single field (e.g. rewriting just the description) costs **0.5 credits**. Enhancing an image costs **10 credits**. As a rough guide: the Starter plan (250 credits) suits stores adding up to ~80 new products per month. The Standard plan (1,500 credits) suits catalogs adding 300–500 products monthly. Additional credit top-up packs are available at any time.


Will the AI-generated content be unique? Will Google penalise it?
+

Yes, every listing is uniquely generated. Listagrow is a **visual-first AI** \- it analyses the specific colours, textures, shapes, and details in your product photos, not generic text templates. Two different products will always produce fundamentally different descriptions. Your brand guidelines and any optional product notes you add act as a unique input layer that further differentiates your content. Google rewards unique, accurate product descriptions - our output is engineered to meet exactly that standard.

Can I use Listagrow to improve my existing product catalog?
+

Absolutely. The **Catalog Enrichment** feature lets you import any existing product CSV (regardless of platform). Listagrow's AI maps the fields automatically, then identifies products with missing descriptions, weak titles, absent SEO metadata, or off-brand language. You can then enrich products in bulk, bringing your entire catalog up to the same quality standard without starting from scratch.


Can I generate product listings from photos taken on my phone?
+

Yes - absolutely. Listagrow is designed to work with any image quality, including photos taken on a smartphone. The AI analyses your image for visual details (colour, texture, shape, context) rather than relying on camera quality metadata. Practically, this means you can snap a product photo at a trade show, in your stockroom, or while unpacking a new supplier delivery, upload it on your phone browser, and have a complete on-brand listing generated in under a minute. For the best AI image enhancement results, aim for good natural lighting and a plain background, but even basic phone shots produce strong listing copy.


Does Listagrow generate platform-specific product content?
+

Yes. Every e-commerce platform has different content requirements - Shopify expects concise SEO titles and benefit-led descriptions, Amazon requires structured bullet points and keyword-rich backend fields, Etsy rewards story-driven copy with specific material details, and so on. In your Brand & Content Settings you choose a platform target and define your preferred content structure (features, benefits, specifications, materials, care instructions, etc.). Listagrow's AI then generates content that conforms to the correct format, character limits, and field structure for that platform. If you export for multiple platforms, you can create different brand profiles for each.


How does Listagrow help my products appear in AI chatbot results?
+

AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity increasingly surface product recommendations from indexed e-commerce content. They favour listings that are complete, well-structured, and naturally keyword-rich - the exact output Listagrow generates. Specifically: descriptive, semantically rich titles help AI models understand _what_ your product is; complete descriptions with materials, use cases, and dimensions help match buyer intent queries; structured metadata and accurate alt text improve overall page comprehension. Listagrow's upcoming SEO Keyword Research feature will go further - researching the exact phrases buyers use in AI chatbot queries and weaving them into your listings automatically.
