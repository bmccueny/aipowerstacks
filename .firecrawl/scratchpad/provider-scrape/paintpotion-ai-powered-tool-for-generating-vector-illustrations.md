# Dear Paint Potion Users,

I launched this project one year ago on my own. Since then, I've become involved in other projects that are progressing more successfully and where I am not a solo founder, making them easier to manage.

Consequently, I have made the difficult decision to discontinue Paint Potion.

I want to sincerely thank everyone who used Paint Potion. I am glad if it proved to be a useful tool in your creative endeavors.

For those looking for a similar service, you might find [exactly.ai](https://exactly.ai/) to be a helpful alternative, as they are working on similar solutions.

If you have images or data you wish to retrieve, please access your account at [app.paint-potion.com](https://app.paint-potion.com/) before **June 5th**. After this date, the platform will no longer be accessible.

Thank you for being a part of the Paint Potion journey.
