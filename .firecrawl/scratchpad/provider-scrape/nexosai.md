## Frequently asked questions

### What is nexos.ai?

nexos.ai is an AI orchestration platform designed to help enterprises streamline the adoption and management of large language models (LLMs). It provides a robust API layer that integrates multiple AI models so that businesses can efficiently select, manage, and switch between models based on their requirements. Our AI orchestration platform ensures you can manage AI models easily, improve performance, and keep costs and security in check — all with one platform.

### How does AI orchestration work?

AI orchestration brings structure and control to enterprise AI by unifying integration and management into a single workflow. Here’s how it works:

- **AI integration** Connect multiple LLMs and AI providers through a single API. This eliminates the need for separate integrations and gives your teams access to the tools they need — from OpenAI to Anthropic to privately hosted models.

- **AI automation** Automate core AI workflows like model selection, fallback handling, routing, and multi-step agent tasks. Reduce manual overhead and ensure requests always get the best available response.

- **AI management** Gain full visibility into AI usage across your organization. With guardrails, cost tracking, access controls, and observability, you can manage everything AI-related from one platform.


Together, these capabilities let businesses adopt and scale AI with flexibility and confidence.

### How can businesses benefit from AI platform?

An AI platform enables businesses to harness the power of AI to drive efficiency. By automating repetitive tasks, uncovering actionable insights from large datasets, personalizing customer experiences, and optimizing operations, AI platform helps an organization improve productivity and agility. Unified AI platform reduces AI costs and helps adapt more quickly to changing market conditions.

### What models can I use with nexos.ai?

nexos.ai supports hundreds of LLMs from top platforms like Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, Mistral, Meta, and Grok. Our AI gateway also works with locally or privately hosted models on on-premises setups, Ollama, and more.

### How does nexos.ai integrate with existing applications?

Integrating nexos.ai is easy — just update your app’s API base path to our endpoint instead of OpenAI’s. This automatically routes all LLM requests through our platform for better management and optimization.

### What if I already have a deal with an LLM provider?

No problem — you can still take full advantage of nexos.ai. Just plug in your existing API key for providers like OpenAI and continue using your deal while benefiting from our orchestration, guardrails, and observability features.

### How does nexos.ai support internal security policies?

With audit logs, AI guardrails, user-level access controls, and private model hosting options, nexos.ai will help you enforce your organization's security requirements.
