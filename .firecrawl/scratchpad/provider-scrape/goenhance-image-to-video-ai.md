## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is GoEnhance AI?

GoEnhance AI is an all-in-one AI video platform that makes creating professional videos easier than ever before. Its suite of tools includes AI-powered video-to-anime conversion, which offers exceptional consistency and high resolution( 4k,60fps ). Our platform also features text/image to video, video face swap technology, text to image, character animation capabilities, and more.

With this comprehensive set of features, GoEnhance AI empowers users to produce high-quality video content efficiently and creatively.

### Is GoEnhance AI suitable for beginners?

GoEnhance AI is designed to be accessible and user-friendly for creators of all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you are a professional creator or a beginner, you can use our all functions very easily. Our platform is available on both PC and mobile devices, ensuring you can create and enhance your content wherever you are, making it even more convenient for users of all experience levels.

### Can I use GoEnhance AI for free? What benefits do I get from a paid subscription?

Absolutely! GoEnhance AI offers a free tier that includes some tokens. These can be used for all features within our platform.

Our paid subscriptions come with extra benefits: an increased token allowance, faster generation, more concurrent jobs, and access to premium features. To find out more about our subscription plans, click the "Upgrade" button at the top right of the page.

### What payment methods does GoEnhance AI accept?

We use stripe to collect payments, with mainstream cards such as Mastercard, Visa, AliPay, etc.

### What are Fast Mode and Relax Mode?

Fast Mode: This mode attempts to provide you with instant GPU access but will cost tokens. It is best suited for tasks that require fast output.

Relax Mode: This mode doesn't cost any tokens. You can create an unlimited number of images and videos each month in Relax Mode only for the features that support it, available to subscribers of the Standard, Pro, and Mega plans. Jobs in Relax Mode may be queued based on system usage, and the speed may be slower. It is best suited for tasks where speed isn't a priority, and it’s only available for specific functions.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by logging into [https://app.goenhance.ai/billing-setting](https://app.goenhance.ai/billing-setting), and clicking the "Manage Subscription" button. This will redirect you to Stripe's billing portal, where you can manage your plan, download invoices, switch plans, or cancel your subscription.

### When Does My Subscription Renew?

Your subscription renews on the same day each month as when you initially subscribed. you can see the details in your Billing & Payment. For annual plans, your credit reset occurs on the same day each month (e.g., if you subscribed on January 12th, your reset time is on the 12th of each month).

### Can I use my creations for both personal and commercial purposes?

Yes, you retain the rights to your creations and can use them for personal purposes. However, commercial usage is only permitted for subscribers of our Standard, Pro, and Mega plans. Free users and Basic plan subscribers are limited to personal use only.

We encourage you to explore the full potential of your projects, but for legal reasons, we kindly request that you refrain from using copyrighted or trademarked material, including but not limited to celebrities, cartoon characters, and other protected entities. This ensures a legal and ethical usage environment for all.

### Refund Policy

We apologize for any inconvenience, but due to the AI-driven enhancer process, which involves significant computational costs, particularly for the GPU processing time required to create videos, we are unable to offer refunds. This policy is in part due to our upstream providers' terms, which do not accommodate refunds for the GPU processing time utilized. Issuing refunds would result in a financial deficit for our company. During the sign-up process, you agreed that the right to a refund is waived for these specific reasons. We appreciate your understanding and encourage you to utilize our trial options to ensure the service meets your needs before purchasing.
