### What if some license plates aren't recognized and not blurred?![avclabs faq](https://www.avclabs.com/assets-new/img/icons/arrow-down.svg)

**Method 1**: Click the Menu button (on the upper right) > Settings, and switch to Model Settings. Scroll down to the License Plate Settings and reduce the Detection Accuracy to detect more license plates.
