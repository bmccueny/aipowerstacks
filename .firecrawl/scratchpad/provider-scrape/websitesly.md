## Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about building, customizing, and
managing
your AI-generated websites on Websites.ly.

## What is Websites.ly?

Websites.ly is an AI-powered platform that lets you turn any idea into a fully functional custom
website — without writing a single line of code.


## Do I need coding experience?

Not at all! Websites.ly is built for everyone. Our AI builder and ready-to-use templates handle
all the code — you just describe what you want.


## Can I customize templates?

Yes! You can select from a variety of templates, then edit layouts, colors, and text directly in
the app to match your brand and style.


## How does AI help me build a website?

The AI chat assistant interacts with you like a human — asking about your goals, preferred
design, and content — then generates a ready-to-use website structure instantly.


## Can I connect my own domain?

Absolutely! You can use a custom domain name (like **domain.com**) or host your
site under a free Websites.ly subdomain.


## Can I edit code directly in the browser?

Yes! Websites.ly includes an in-app code editor that lets you tweak HTML, CSS, or JS directly
without needing any external tools.


## Can I upload images and files?

Yes, you can easily upload and manage your images, icons, and files through the built-in file
manager — all accessible inside your dashboard.


## How much does it cost to start?

Websites.ly offers a free plan so you can start building right away. Paid plans are available if
you want more powerful features.


## What makes Websites.ly different from other builders?

Unlike traditional website builders, Websites.ly uses the power of AI to design, write, and edit
content based on your prompts — making it faster and more intuitive than manual tools.


## What makes Websites.ly better than other AI website builders?

Websites.ly stands out with its true all-in-one experience — combining AI website generation,
real-time AI chat interaction, premade templates to choose from, built-in code editor, file
manager, and custom domain support.
You can go from idea to live website in minutes, without leaving the platform.
