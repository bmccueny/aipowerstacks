## Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose EchoWrite over a monthly subscription?

Most apps charge you $20/month to "wrap" an API that costs pennies. EchoWrite is a pro tool: you pay for the software once, and you control your data and costs.

How "local" is the local mode?

Completely. When using local models, EchoWrite functions without an internet connection. Your GPU/CPU handles the heavy lifting, ensuring your data never touches the wire.

Do Custom Prompts and Translation work offline?

**Yes — fully.** EchoWrite includes built-in local LLMs (Llama 3.2 3B and Mistral 7B) that run entirely on your device. When "Text Processing (AI)" is set to Local, Custom Prompts and Translation work with no internet connection and no API key required. You can also use OpenAI's API for maximum quality — the choice is yours, and both pipelines are configured independently.

Do I need my own OpenAI API Key?

**No — but you can.** EchoWrite supports both modes. Use your own OpenAI key for maximum accuracy (BYOK), or run a local Whisper model completely offline and free. Your choice.

How much does the API cost?

Incredibly affordable. OpenAI's Whisper API costs **$0.006 per minute** — over **2 hours of audio for less than $1**. For typical daily usage, your monthly bill will likely be just a few cents.

Is my voice data private?

**Absolutely.** EchoWrite processes your audio locally to prepare it, then sends it directly to OpenAI using your personal key. Your audio and transcriptions never touch our servers.

Does it work on Mac or Linux?

Currently, EchoWrite is built natively for **Windows 10 and Windows 11**. Mac support is not currently planned.
