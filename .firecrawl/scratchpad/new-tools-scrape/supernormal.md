## How the Supernormal desktop app works

Step1

### Download and set up in minutes

Sign in with Google or your work email. The app walks you through connecting your calendar, granting audio permissions, selecting your language, and answering a few quick setup questions. The whole process takes less than two minutes.

![Supernormal Desktop App onboarding flow showing sign-in, calendar connection, audio permission setup, language selection, and quick setup steps.](https://framerusercontent.com/images/kivCTpClqOUWPjhcuN9l75GE.webp?width=2382&height=2190)

![Supernormal Desktop App capturing meeting audio in the background, transcribing all speakers, and creating a complete meeting record without a bot joining the call.](https://framerusercontent.com/images/H7xcKUOnpuSsblR84KPVD8gsLI.webp?width=2399&height=2190)

Step2

### Supernormal captures your meetings automatically

When a meeting starts, the Supernormal App captures the audio from your computer (no bot joins the call). It transcribes all voices and generates a complete meeting record. The app runs quietly in the background so you stay focused on your meeting.

Step3

### Personalized suggestions after every meeting

After your meeting, open the Supernormal web app to see personalized suggestions based on what was discussed. Click any suggestion to generate a draft instantly (follow-up emails, documents, calendar invites, and more). Move these into your work tools with one click to complete your tasks in seconds.

![Supernormal web app showing personalized post-meeting suggestions after clicking the Desktop App button to open your meeting in Supernormal, where you can instantly generate follow-up emails, documents, calendar invites, and more.](https://framerusercontent.com/images/UWWqZSeLxEkV2jgNvKn9K48VS3E.webp?width=2382&height=2190)
