Introducing Lightfield

**Traditional CRMs expect you to log notes, update fields, and draft follow-ups.** Instead of bringing you closer to your customers, they drown you in busywork and put you out of touch with what’s actually happening in your company.

That's why we built Lightfield — the first AI-native CRM with complete customer memory. It updates itself after every meeting you have, drives next steps for you, and can answer any question about your business.

[Watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Idmapqa9vYk)

Foundations

## **Full customer context to build**  **and scale your company**

- ### Every customer interaction in context



Lightfield reads emails, meeting transcripts, and other conversation records you share with it to compile an exhaustive history of your customer relationships.

- ### A world model for your business



Lightfield develops contextual understanding of your company, your product, and your market. This gives agents comprehensive context when they answer questions or perform tasks for you.

- ### Schema-less foundation



No upfront configuration required - Lightfield captures everything from day 1 and lets you evolve your data model over time.
