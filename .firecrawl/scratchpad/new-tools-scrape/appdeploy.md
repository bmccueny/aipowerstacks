## Do I need to know how to host an app?

No. Building apps with AI is incredibly fast, but getting them live is still a bottleneck.
AppDeploy removes the entire setup process so you can stay purely focused on your product.

- →No hosting providers to evaluate
- →No database or authentication setup
- →No complex deployment pipelines
