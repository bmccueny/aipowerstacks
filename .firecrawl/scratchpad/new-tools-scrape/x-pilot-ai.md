## How to create course videos with AI

Upload any document, and X-Pilot turns it into a publish-ready video course — in five steps, zero editing skills.

1. Upload your PDFs, PPTs, or Markdown files. X-Pilot's AI analyzes document structure and extracts key concepts, then transforms them into professional course videos with knowledge visualization.

2. See your video taking shape in real-time as X-Pilot processes your content with Visual Motion Boxes. Review the AI-generated outline, chapter structure, animations, and voiceover before finalizing.

3. Edit your video by simply describing what you want — no timeline, no complex tools. Type commands like "Make the intro shorter" or "Add emphasis at 2:30" and X-Pilot applies changes instantly. 36x faster than traditional video editing.

4. Export in 1080p or 4K with no watermark. Package as SCORM for LMS deployment, or translate to 160+ languages while preserving knowledge visualization, voiceover sync, and brand styling.

5. Share directly to Udemy, Coursera, YouTube, or any SCORM-compatible LMS. Download for offline distribution or embed on your own website. No credit card required to start.

## Frequently asked questions

Common questions about X-Pilot's AI course video maker — pricing, editing, accuracy, and more.

### Can X-Pilot turn my PDF, PPT, or Word doc into a video course?

Yes. Upload a document, and X-Pilot breaks it into chapters, generates a narrated script, and adds animated visuals for each concept. The entire process takes minutes, not days. [Learn more about PDF to video conversion](https://www.x-pilot.ai/products/pdf-to-video-knowledge).

### How is X-Pilot different from Sora or HeyGen?

Sora generates cinematic footage, HeyGen generates talking-head avatars. X-Pilot generates structured course videos grounded in your own content — every formula, diagram, and data point stays accurate. [See detailed comparisons](https://www.x-pilot.ai/compare).

### How does X-Pilot avoid AI hallucinations?

Every visual is generated directly from your source material, not imagined by a model. You can preview and edit every frame before publishing — nothing goes out without your approval.

### What are Visual Motion Boxes?

Animated visual components — infographics, flowcharts, diagrams, code walkthroughs — that X-Pilot generates to illustrate concepts in your video. They replace static slides and stock footage with purpose-built visuals tied to your content.

### Can I edit the generated video?

Yes, everything is editable. Change the script, swap visuals, adjust voiceover pacing, or update branding. You can use natural-language commands or edit manually — it works like a video editor, not a one-shot generator. [Try the Natural Language Editor](https://www.x-pilot.ai/products/natural-language-video-editor).

### Can I create full-length courses, not just short clips?

Yes. X-Pilot handles 1–20 minute lessons and multi-module course series. Upload a long document, and the system auto-segments it into chapters with consistent style and narration across the entire course.

### Who uses X-Pilot?

[Online course creators](https://www.x-pilot.ai/solutions/course-creation), [corporate L&D teams](https://www.x-pilot.ai/solutions/corporate-training), university faculty, consultants, and professionals in [finance](https://www.x-pilot.ai/solutions/fintech), [healthcare](https://www.x-pilot.ai/solutions/healthcare), and tech who need accuracy in their training materials.

### How much time does it save compared to traditional video production?

Most users report 10–20x faster production. A 10-minute course video that would take a week with After Effects and a voiceover artist can be done in under an hour.

### Does X-Pilot support AI voiceover and voice cloning?

Yes. Pick from natural-sounding AI voices in multiple languages, or clone your own voice so every lesson sounds like you. Pronunciation and pacing are fully adjustable.

### Do I own the videos I create?

Yes, 100%. You hold full commercial rights. Sell on Udemy, publish on YouTube, embed in your LMS, or use for internal training — no attribution or royalties required.

### Is X-Pilot free?

Yes. The free Creator plan includes 3 minutes of AI video generation per month — enough to test the full workflow. No credit card required. [Paid plans](https://www.x-pilot.ai/price) start at $9/month for more minutes and features like 4K export and voice cloning.

### Do I need video editing skills to use X-Pilot?

No. X-Pilot is designed for subject-matter experts, not video editors. Upload your document, review the AI-generated course video, and refine with plain-English commands. There is no timeline, no layers, and no rendering queue.

### Can I sell courses made with X-Pilot on Udemy or Teachable?

Yes. You own 100% commercial rights to every video you create. Export as MP4 and upload directly to Udemy, Teachable, Thinkific, Coursera, or any other platform. Many of our users build and sell full course catalogs this way. [See how Udemy & Teachable creators use X-Pilot](https://www.x-pilot.ai/solutions/udemy-teachable-course-creators).

### Can I create a course without being on camera?

Yes — that's what X-Pilot is built for. Upload a document or script, and X-Pilot generates animated course videos with AI narration. No camera, no recording studio, no screen recording. Your content becomes the visuals. [Try the free AI course generator](https://www.x-pilot.ai/products/free-ai-course-generator).

Yes. Small businesses and L&D teams use X-Pilot to convert SOPs, handbooks, and onboarding docs into standardized training video modules. Export as SCORM for LMS deployment or MP4 for direct sharing.
