## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can you afford to provide unlimited access to all these models and tools?

ZeroTwo is paid for, marketed, coded and sold by a single person. We don't have any investors or VC funding, so we can keep our prices low and offer unlimited access to all our features and models.

### Why choose ZeroTwo over ChatGPT or Claude?

ChatGPT and Claude are both great platforms and offer a variety of tools and models. The issue is that they serve different purposes. Sometimes you need a coding model to build your product or sometimes you need an image generation or video model to create content. ZeroTwo is for users who need access to both but don't want to pay for multiple subscriptions.

### Who is ZeroTwo for?

ZeroTwo is for anyone who wants to work smarter without having to pay for multiple subscriptions. It's for students, professionals, founders, creators, and anyone who wants to use AI to their advantage.

### Can I use ZeroTwo with my existing apps?

Yes! ZeroTwo integrates with popular tools via MCP (Model Connector Protocol) and our own connectors (MCP but with oauth). This means you can pull in data or work with your existing tools and data within ZeroTwo.

### What's included in the Pro plan?

The Pro plan gives you unlimited model usage, unlimited projects, and unlimited access to all the tools (image gen, code interpeter, exa neural search, deep research, etc.). The one exception to this is video generation which is limited to 1 video generation for now. Eventually we plan to raise limits for video generation, but were still working on it.

### Is there a free plan available?

Yes, ZeroTwo offers a free plan with limited access to models and tools. The free plan is limited to 40 messages per day, 1 file upload per message, 1 project, and 1 video generation. The free plan is a great way to get started with ZeroTwo and see if it's right for you.

### Is my data secure with ZeroTwo?

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use enterprise-grade security measures including encryption and secure data handling. Your conversations and projects remain private to you and only those you choose to share them with. None of your data is trained on by any of the AI models (with the exception of a few models such as deepseek and qwen which we clarify in the model selector). Unlike competitors we also don't analyze your data for product improvements (however we do review messages which have been rated as helpful or non-helpful).
