## Questions We Hear.

What are AI agents?

Think of them as specialized team members — a Lead Scout that finds prospects, an Outreach Writer that crafts personalized sequences, an Inbound Agent that catches replies and callbacks, a Caller that books meetings. They work together automatically, passing data and context to each other like a real sales team.

How is Cora different from other AI sales tools?

Most AI sales tools give you another dashboard to manage. Cora gives you a conversation. Tell her what you need, she builds the agents, runs the pipeline, and only interrupts you when something needs your judgment.

Will my prospects know it’s AI?

Cora learns your voice and style. Every touchpoint — email, call, LinkedIn message — sounds like your best rep. We don’t do generic templates.

How quickly can I get started?

In minutes. There’s no setup wizard, no CSV upload, no workflow builder to configure. You talk to Cora, approve her plan, and your AI workforce starts working.

What channels does Cora support?

Email, phone calls, SMS, and LinkedIn. Cora coordinates across all channels so your outreach feels natural and consistent, not spammy.

Can I control the messaging and tone?

Absolutely. Cora learns your voice from examples you provide and adapts to your brand guidelines. Every message she sends sounds like your best rep wrote it — because the style comes from you.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, and your lead data never leaves secure servers. All communications comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and TCPA regulations.
