## Frequently Asked Questions

Is kikivoice really free?

Yes, we offer a free tier to experience core features. Free experience credit points reset weekly, and credits are consumed during conversion. No registration or credit card required, allowing you to start creating immediately.


What audio formats are supported for voice cloning?

AI voice cloning requires you to upload audio files (you can record directly or upload audio/video files). We support mainstream audio formats: WAV, MP3, OGG, OPUS, AAC, M4A, FLAC, WMA, ALAC, AIFF, AMR, as well as video formats: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, WEBM, etc. (maximum 50MB). After cloning conversion is complete, export and download formats support 5 types: MP3, WAV, OGG, AAC, OPUS. Audio quality can be selected as standard or high quality, with unlimited downloads and unlimited playback.
