Why it exists

## Personal context is the key to _performance_

The best advice requires the deepest context. A great coach doesn't give generic frameworks — they know your patterns, your relationships, your blind spots. They see what you can't.

That's what Kin builds. Not a chatbot. Not a search engine. A persistent, evolving understanding of who you are — so every conversation starts where the last one ended.

The result? Support that actually fits your life. Decisions that account for your full picture. Clarity when everything feels noisy.

What it's built around

## Your mind is not a _product_

The world is getting more connected. Personal data is growing faster than computation can keep up. That opens the door to something truly personal — but only if the individual controls it. That's the future we're building toward.

### End-to-end encrypted

Your conversations are encrypted at rest and in transit. We can't read them. Nobody can.

### No model training

Your data never trains AI models. Not ours, not anyone's. Your thoughts stay yours.

### On-device processing

Core intelligence runs locally. Your most sensitive data never leaves your device.

### Full data control

Export or delete everything, anytime. You own your data. Period.
