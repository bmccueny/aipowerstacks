## Frequently Asked Questions

What is click fraud and how does it affect my ad spend?

Click fraud is when bots, competitors, or click farms repeatedly click your paid ads with no intent to buy. It inflates your cost-per-click, drains your daily budget faster, and corrupts your conversion data — making it impossible to optimize campaigns accurately. Studies estimate 14-20% of all paid clicks are fraudulent.

How do I get a refund from Google Ads for invalid clicks?

Google automatically filters some invalid clicks, but many slip through. To claim a manual refund, you need to submit an Invalid Clicks Contact Form with evidence: timestamps, IP patterns, and behavioral proof that clicks were non-human. Ghostwall's Recovery plan generates this evidence automatically in court-ready PDF format.

How much does click fraud cost advertisers?

Click fraud costs advertisers an estimated $100 billion globally per year. For individual businesses, 14-20% of ad spend typically goes to fraudulent clicks. On a $10,000/month ad budget, that's $1,400-$2,000 wasted every month on fake traffic that will never convert.

How does Ghostwall detect bots?

Ghostwall uses 50+ behavioral signals to distinguish humans from bots: mouse movement patterns, scroll behavior, keystroke dynamics, touch pressure, hardware fingerprinting, and timing analysis. Unlike IP blocklists, our sensor detects sophisticated bots that mimic human behavior — achieving 95%+ detection accuracy.

Which ad platforms does Ghostwall work with?

Ghostwall works with all major ad platforms including Google Ads, Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram), Microsoft Ads (Bing), LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, and X (Twitter) Ads. Our evidence exports are formatted specifically for each platform's refund claim process.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes under 5 minutes. Add a single JavaScript snippet to your site — no code changes to your existing pages, no server configuration, no developer needed. You'll see bot detection results within minutes of installation.
