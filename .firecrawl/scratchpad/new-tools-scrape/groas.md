## How It Works

groas is NOT another marketing 'tool' to make humans better at their job. It's a replacement for the job entirely.

The best media buyer in the world looks at maybe 50 data points before making a decision. groas processes over 100,000 daily - then acts on them autonomously, scaling your campaigns to performance levels humans physically cannot reach.
