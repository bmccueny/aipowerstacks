# Your Chats can Access Past Conversations, Files & Agents

Simply click and drag to connect chats to anything in your workspace. Everything saves to the workspace.

Dozens of chats visible at once.Have multiple ongoing conversations at once, each with different chat configurations.

Everything connects.Connect a chat to a file, knowledge base or agent to give access to the context.

Read, analyze and edit.When connected to a file, the chat can read, analyze and edit the file. File types allowed on the canvas: PDF, images, videos, word (docx), markdown (md), text (txt)
