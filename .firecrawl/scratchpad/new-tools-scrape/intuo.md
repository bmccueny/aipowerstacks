## Questions

Does it actually sound like me, or like AI?

IntuoAI builds a voice profile from your existing LinkedIn content, writing samples,
and the answers you give during setup. Posts are written to match your tone, vocabulary,
and the way you argue a point. If a post doesn't sound right, you edit or regenerate it
before it goes out.


Where does the content come from?

We monitor X/Twitter accounts and industry sources relevant to your field, then use that
to ground posts in real, current events — not hallucinated trends. You choose which
accounts to track and can add your own ideas to the mix.


Can I review posts before they go live?

Yes — every post sits in your queue before publishing. You approve, skip, or edit each one.
IntuoAI only publishes what you've approved. Nothing goes out automatically without
your sign-off.


How long does setup take?

The onboarding flow takes about 5 minutes. Connect LinkedIn, tell us about your
business and audience, and IntuoAI generates your first batch of posts. You can
start publishing the same day.
