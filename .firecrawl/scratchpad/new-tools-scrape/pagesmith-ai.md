## Frequently Asked Questions

Will my site show up on Google?

Yes. Unlike most AI builders that create sites invisible to search engines, Pagesmith generates real HTML pages that Google can read and index immediately. Your content is visible in the source code, metadata is properly structured, and there's nothing blocking search engines from finding you.

How fast are Pagesmith sites?

Extremely fast. Your pages load in milliseconds because they're built as lightweight HTML — no heavy scripts slowing things down. Most Pagesmith sites score 95-100 on Google PageSpeed without any optimization work from you.

Do I own my website's code?

Yes, 100%. Unlike other builders that lock you into their platform, Pagesmith gives you a standard Astro/React repository. You can export it, host it anywhere, or hand it to a developer to extend manually. It's your intellectual property.

Do I need coding skills?

No. You build by chatting with our AI, which acts as your senior developer. However, because the output is real code, developers love using Pagesmith too — they can inspect and modify the underlying codebase if they want to.

Will AI tools like ChatGPT cite my site?

AI search engines cite structured, authoritative content. Pagesmith generates pages with semantic HTML, JSON-LD schema markup, and clear content structure. This gives AI tools the signals they need to understand and reference your site in their answers.

Can I add forms, databases, or dynamic features?

Yes. Pagesmith isn't just for static brochures. Every project includes a SQLite database, server-side rendering when needed, and React components for interactive features. Build contact forms, reservation systems, member areas, or calculators — all while keeping the SEO benefits of static pages.

How is Pagesmith different from Lovable or Bolt?

Lovable and Bolt are excellent for building web apps — dashboards, internal tools, MVPs. Pagesmith is purpose-built for public-facing websites where SEO matters. We generate static HTML that search engines can index immediately, not client-side React apps that require JavaScript to render content.

Can I migrate my existing WordPress site?

Yes. Paste your WordPress URL and Pagesmith will analyze your content, design, and structure to generate a modern static replacement. You get the same site without the plugin maintenance, security updates, or database overhead. Most migrations take minutes, not weeks.

Where is my site hosted?

Your site is deployed to Cloudflare's global edge network with automatic SSL, DDoS protection, and caching. You can also export to GitHub and deploy anywhere you want — Vercel, Netlify, or your own infrastructure. Custom domains are supported on all plans.
