## Frequently asked questions

Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? [Reach out](mailto:support@paraspeech.com) and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.

Does Paraspeech require an internet connection?Only the initial model download requires an internet connection. Paraspeech is fully offline after that. All transcription happens locally on your Mac for speed and privacy.

Will Paraspeech slow down my Mac?Paraspeech is optimized for Apple Silicon and is extremely lightweight. It runs efficiently in the background without impacting performance. It uses <200MB of RAM and only uses CPU/GPU when transcribing.

Which apps does Paraspeech work with?Every app. Paraspeech works system‑wide—editors, email, browsers, docs, and chat apps—no plugins or extra setup required.

Is my voice data private?Yes. Audio and text never leave your device. Your recordings or transcriptions are never uploaded or stored on external servers.

What hardware do I need?Paraspeech is built for Apple Silicon (M‑series), but it will work even on 8GB M1 Macs.

How does the trial work?You get a number of free transcriptions to try it out. The app shows how many you have left. After that, you can purchase a license—no subscription required.

What languages does Paraspeech support?Paraspeech supports 100+ languages with the Multilingual model. There's also a dedicated English-only model for even better accuracy if you only speak English.

Can I use Paraspeech on multiple Macs?Each lifetime license covers one Mac. If you need Paraspeech on additional Macs, you can purchase extra licenses.

How do I use Paraspeech?Hold your shortcut key (Control by default), speak, and release. Your words appear instantly wherever your cursor is—no pasting required.

What is AI Rewriting?AI Rewriting cleans up and polishes your transcriptions using on-device AI. Set up templates for different apps—use an email template in Gmail, a casual tone in Slack, or create custom templates. Paraspeech automatically applies the right template based on which app you're using.
