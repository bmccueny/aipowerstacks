### Frequently Asked Questions

How does Auto Apply work?

Once you create your profile and upload your resume, the JobGPT agent searches through job listings across various platforms for the most relevant jobs. It then automatically fills out application forms on your behalf, attaches your resume, and even crafts personalized cover letters tailored to each job and company.

Its like your very own conceirge for jobs.

How is this different from Linkedin Easy Apply?

Linkedin provides Easy Apply option to only a handful of select jobs. JobGPT, however, can auto-apply to most of the jobs that do not have the Linkedin Easy Apply option, saving you the trouble of applying to them manually.

Is it free to use?

Yes, you can apply upto 5 jobs for free. Beyond that, it works on credit system where 1 credit = 1 job

How many jobs does it apply daily?

It applies to 10-20 jobs on a daily basis. This is to ensure the latest job openings are prioritized first and your credits are not spent on older job postings.

What about remote jobs?

Just configure JobGPT to look for remote openings & it will only apply to remote opportunities for you.

I need a job fast. Can I apply to 100 jobs in a day?

Absolutely! You can choose the Apex Hunter plan that will try to apply to as many jobs as possible within a day for you.

How do I know if a job has been actually applied?

You will get a confirmation email from the company website upon a successful application. You can even track the jobs status from the Jobhunt dashboard
