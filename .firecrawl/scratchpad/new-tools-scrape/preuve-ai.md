## The questions founders ask before they trust the score.

How does the AI actually work?

This isn't a ChatGPT prompt. AI agents search 40+ live sources in parallel, pull real data (competitors, funding, trends), filter out junk, and feed verified facts to a scoring model. Every claim links to a source.

What do I get in the free scan?

A viability score, the main risks dragging your idea down, 2 competitor previews, and a clear go/no-go verdict.

Can I iterate on my idea after getting my report?

Yes. Tweak your positioning, re-run the analysis, see your score change. Many founders iterate 2-3 times before they nail it.

Are there packs for multiple reports?

Yes: 5 reports for $89 ($17.80 each) or 10 reports for $149 ($14.90 each). Perfect if you're testing multiple ideas or want to iterate with fresh data.

Which sources are scanned?

Community discussions, product directories, search trends, public pricing pages, and other live market signals relevant to your idea.

What's your refund policy?

Reports come with a 7-day money-back guarantee - if it didn't help, email us for a full refund, no questions asked. Subscriptions (Radar, Builder) can be canceled anytime from your account with no commitment or cancellation fees.
