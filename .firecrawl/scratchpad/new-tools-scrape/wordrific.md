## Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about Wordrific.

How does Wordrific work?

Paste your website URL and we scan it to build your ICP, brand voice, and content strategy automatically. Every morning, fresh articles are generated and placed in your dashboard for approval. Approve, tweak, or enable auto-publish. That's it.

What is an ICP and why does it matter?

ICP stands for Ideal Customer Profile. It's a detailed description of your perfect customer including their company size, industry, pain points, and buying behavior. Wordrific uses your ICP to generate content that resonates with exactly the right audience, leading to higher engagement and conversion rates.

How does the AI generate content in my brand voice?

During onboarding, you can provide examples of your existing content or describe your brand's tone and style. Our AI analyzes these inputs to learn your unique voice, ensuring every piece of content sounds authentically like your brand.

Which platforms can I publish to?

Wordrific generates optimized content for any platform: LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Instagram, Reddit, and more. For direct auto-publishing, we currently integrate with WordPress, Webflow, Medium, and Ghost, with more platforms coming soon based on customer feedback.

Can I edit the AI-generated content before publishing?

Absolutely! Every piece of content goes through your approval before publishing. You can edit using our rich text editor, request AI tweaks, or set up autopilot mode for hands-off publishing with your pre-approved guidelines.

How many articles can I generate?

It depends on your plan: Starter gets 1 article per day (~30/month), Pro gets 3 per day (~90/month), and Enterprise gets 5 per day (~150/month). All articles go through our full 6-stage pipeline for maximum quality.

Will the content rank on Google?

Our AI is specifically trained for SEO best practices. Every piece includes optimized meta descriptions, keyword placement, proper heading structure, and readability optimization. Our customers typically see first-page rankings within 2-3 months of consistent publishing.

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI writers?

Wordrific is a proprietary AI system purpose-built for content marketing, not a generic chatbot wrapper. We combine ICP research, trend analysis, SEO optimization, brand voice learning, and multi-platform publishing into one seamless workflow. You're not just getting AI writing; you're getting a complete content engine with specialized models trained specifically for marketing performance.

What if I already have a content team?

Wordrific amplifies your team's output, not replaces them. Your content strategists can focus on high-level planning while Wordrific handles research, first drafts, and distribution. Teams typically 5-10x their output without adding headcount.

Is my content and data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, never train AI models on your data, and comply with GDPR and SOC 2 requirements. Your content and customer data remain completely private and secure.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer team plans or enterprise pricing?

Yes! Our Pro plan supports up to 5 team members, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited seats, dedicated support, custom integrations, and volume discounts. Contact us for a custom quote.
