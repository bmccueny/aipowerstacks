The Modern Challange

## AI doesn't know what **your team** knows.

Every time someone prompts AI, they start from scratch.

So outputs miss the mark, work gets repeated, and teams spend more time fixing AI than using it.

illumi is where your team captures that knowledge before anyone hits generate. When AI works from your actual context, outputs get grounded in what your team already knows.

**In the AI era, good context layer pays off within hours, not quarters.**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68823c35fdcdf60e123f2a76/6991bbe11855d2e25fa72b91_feature01-visualworkspace.webp)

### Gather context before you generate

**_Problem:_** _Teams jump straight to AI generation without shared preparation_

With illumi, assemble your context first. Upload files, paste Slack threads, structure your thinking on an infinite canvas. When you hit "generate," AI sees everything your team discussed—not just the final prompt.

**Result:** Stop recreating context. AI gets complete inputs and outputs that actually match your situation.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68823c35fdcdf60e123f2a76/6991bcbe1372d0661b572406_feature02-gkm.webp)

### One place for the full collaboration lifecycle

**_Problem:_** _Context breaks every time your team switches tools. Planning happens in Figma, decisions in Slack, execution in docs, and knowledge in Notion. By the time a project ends, the thinking that produced the work is gone. The next team inherits the deliverable but not the reasoning._

With illumi, your team moves from brainstorming → alignment → AI co-creation → knowledge capture without switching tools. Context persists across the full lifecycle.

**Result:** 10x faster team alignment, with the reasoning behind decisions as accessible as the decisions themselves.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68823c35fdcdf60e123f2a76/6991bbe169c0eb5ce43f5e12_feature04-models.webp)

### Every AI model, one shared workspace

**_Problem:_** _Your team shouldn't have to choose between the right model and shared context._

Claude for writing, GPT-4 for code, Gemini for research, each in a different tab. Every switch breaks the shared context your team just built. In illumi, your team accesses multiple AI models on the same canvas; your strategic thinker workflow can use Claude while your image maker uses Nano Banana. Context stays connected and your team stays synced. No one is rebuilding what someone else already figured out in a separate tab.

**Result:** The right model for the right task, with one source of truth for all AI work.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68823c35fdcdf60e123f2a76/6991bc70436757eac7b3989d_feature03-multippl.webp)

### Knowledge that compounds, not resets

**_Problem:_** _Every project starts from scratch. Team wisdom lives in Slack threads, forgotten meeting notes, and individual memories_

In illumi, every workflow, prompt template, and decision becomes reusable team knowledge. New hires inherit what worked, and projects build on past learning. The team that documents well today is the team that moves fastest in six months.

**Result:** 80% time saved turning messy group input into clear outcomes, with organizational knowledge that compounds instead of evaporating.
