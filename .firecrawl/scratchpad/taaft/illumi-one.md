## Overview

Socials: [![facebook](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/facebook.svg)](https://www.facebook.com/illumione)[![linkedin](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/linkedin.svg)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/illumione)[![youtube](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/youtube.svg)](https://www.youtube.com/@illumione)[![discord](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/discord.svg)](https://discord.gg/F8Y7sTVeeP)

illumi is the Context Layer for AI-assisted knowledge work. It provides a multiplayer canvas where teams integrate frontier language / image / reasoning models into their existing processes.

Most AI agents suffer because context remains trapped in people's head or individual tools. illumi moves these interactions onto a shared visual board where teams map ideas and run parallel workflows in real-time. This visual scope gives every collaborator access to the persistent context behind an AI response, which reduces the friction of scattered, ephemeral workflows.

In this workspace, you can build and reuse Prompts and Notes as a collective knowledge asset. Sharing these components creates a library of internal Workflows that standardizes your project intelligence. By capturing insights directly on the board, the platform ensures that your team's knowledge accumulates instead of disappearing when the session ends.

As we move toward agentic AI, this knowledge layer converts directly into Agent Skills. Domain experts can capture their unique know-how and transform it into executable tech assets. This approach allows your team to define the Living Laws that guide your agents, ensuring that every automated action is anchored in your team's specific, professional standards.
