## Overview

EzEditor's AI Magic Editor is a powerful AI tool that edits and enhances photos using text prompts. This browser-based tool allows you to perform actions such as adding objects, replacing clothes, or expanding images without the need for manual masking.

The tool can understand and contextualize complex instructions like 'add a vase on the table', and accurately locates the specified area in the photograph.

It is capable of recognizing and replacing elements like clothing and weather conditions on a photograph by simply typing something like 'change red shirt to blue suit' or 'make it a sunset'.

Additionally, EzEditor also offers the possibility to extend the background of a photo, a feature called outpainting, to resize it to fit various aspect ratios, perfect for social media posts.

The tool provides a user-friendly interface, and other than uploading the image and describing what changes you want, no other action is required from the user.

The algorithms of the tool ensure that all added or changed elements match the lighting, shadows, and perspective of the original photo, delivering high-quality results.
