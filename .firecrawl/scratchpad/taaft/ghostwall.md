## Overview

Ghostwall is an AI tool designed to protect your ad budget from fraudulent activities such as click fraud. It operates across various platforms including Google Ads, Meta (formerly Facebook), Microsoft, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

The detection process needs two phases: identifying and blocking. The tool monitors visitor behavior and identifies patterns that deviate from those of genuine human users, this is essential in detecting bot traffic.

Aside from monitoring and detecting suspicious activities with a level of accuracy, Ghostwall is implemented to shadow-ban these suspicious activities to maintain the integrity of your pixel data.

Another integral part of Ghostwall's functionality is the recovery phase. For every blocked bot event, the tool logs it with the necessary forensic evidence.

This evidence plays a key role when claiming refunds from the respective ad platforms for the fraudulent clicks your ads received. Ghostwall offers two pricing plans; the 'Blocker' and 'Recovery'.

The 'Blocker' focuses on bot detection and blocking on your website while 'Recovery' offers an additional feature of providing the necessary forensic evidence tools for claiming ad platform refund claims.

The operation of Ghostwall does not require complex technical knowledge and the tool can be easily set up on ghostwall.co.
