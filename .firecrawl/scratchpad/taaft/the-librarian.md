What is It?

TheLibrarian.io is a WhatsApp AI Assistant designed to help Master Your Inbox, Manage Your Schedule, Setup Reminders, Find Anything You Need, Post on Social Media — so you can focus on what truly matters.

It seamlessly integrates with all your Google Apps (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts), LinkedIn, Zoom, Slack, Notion, GitHub and many more.

Top Features

1\. Morning & Pre-Meeting Briefs: start your day with a clear, concise overview of what’s ahead. Your meetings, tasks, and priorities—all in one place. Get valuable Pre-Meeting Briefs before each appointment.

2\. Setup Reminders & Scheduled Tasks: never forget anything important again - just ask Librarian to remind you and when the time comes it will send you a WhatsApp message.

3\. Memories & Facts: it remembers key details about you, like your home or office addresses, default Zoom link, email signature, and more. These smart memories save you time by automating repetitive tasks and making your workflow seamless.

4\. Upload Files & Pictures: share files (PDF, PPT) or images with the assistant to extract information or ask questions. Whether it’s a business card, a conference agenda, or a document, TheLibrarian.io makes it easy to get the answers you need.

Target

This tool is best suited for busy professionals who use WhatsApp and spend a lot of time away from their laptops like Real Estate Agents, Insurance Agents, Founders and SMB Owners.
