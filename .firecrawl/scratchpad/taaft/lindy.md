## Overview

Lindy is an AI assistant designed to simplify and streamline personal and professional tasks. It offers a range of features, including calendar management, email drafting, contract management, task organization, and more.

Lindy can be accessed from anywhere and allows for perfect scheduling by handling the back and forth to find common availabilities. The tool pre-drafts email replies, personalized with the user’s voice for each recipient, saving time and streamlining email management.

Lindy learns from the user’s inbox to surface the highest-priority emails, triages them, and handles automatic conflict resolutions. Lindy can also participate in meetings and take notes, summarize podcasts, YouTube videos, emails, or web articles, and integrate with favorite tools such as Linear, Gmail, Google Calendar, Twitter, Google Sheets, Salesforce, Twilio, Hubspot, Google Docs, Airtable, Google Drive, Slack, and LinkedIn to provide infinite personalization.

Lindy is suitable for use in various scenarios such as sales, recruiting, marketing, and more. This AI tool aims to put users' lives on autopilot by automating tasks and providing personalized assistance, thereby providing time back to focus on higher value tasks.

## Q&A

What is Lindy?

Lindy is an AI personal assistant designed to automate and streamline personal and professional tasks.

How does Lindy help in automating tasks?

Lindy takes on various tasks, such as calendar management, email drafting, contract management, and task organizing. It uses AI to handle the more mundane, time-consuming tasks, freeing up the user to focus on higher value activities.

What are the features of Lindy?

Lindy's features include perfect scheduling, pre-drafting of emails personalized in the user's voice, detection of high-priority emails, automatic management of conflicts, contract management, daily briefings, meeting note taking, summarization of various types of content, and integration with numerous popular platforms.

Can I access Lindy from any device?

Yes, Lindy can be accessed from any device. It's not a device-specific tool.

How does Lindy manage scheduling?

Lindy manages scheduling by handling the back and forth that comes with trying to find common availabilities. This makes scheduling efficient and hassle-free.

How does Lindy assist in email management?

Lindy assists in email management by pre-drafting email replies, personalized with the user's voice for each recipient. It also learns from the user's inbox to automatically bring up the highest priority emails.

How does Lindy learn from my inbox?

Lindy learns from your inbox by using AI to detect patterns and habits in your email management. It uses this information to prioritize your emails, pre-draft responses, and handle conflicts.

Can Lindy handle conflicts and resolutions?

Yes, Lindy can handle conflicts and resolutions. It triages your emails to handle any potential conflicts automatically.

How does Lindy participate in meetings?

Lindy participates in meetings by taking notes. This saves you the task of doing it yourself, ensuring nothing important is missed.

How does Lindy summarize content?

Lindy has a summarization feature that can summarize podcasts, Youtube videos, emails, or web articles, reducing the time you need to consume them fully.

Which tools can Lindy integrate with?

Lindy can integrate with a variety of tools including Linear, Gmail, Google Calendar, Twitter, Google Sheets, Salesforce, Twilio, Hubspot, Google Docs, Airtable, Google Drive, Slack, and LinkedIn.

In which scenarios can Lindy be used effectively?

Lindy can be used effectively in various scenarios such as sales, recruiting, and marketing. It's flexibility allows it to adapt to several professional contexts.

How does Lindy help in sales, recruiting or marketing tasks?

In sales, Lindy can update your CRM based on meeting recordings. For recruiting, it can handle scheduling aspects with speed and efficiency. In marketing, integration with other tools facilitates campaign management.

What is meant by Lindy putting my life on autopilot?

Lindy putting your life on autopilot refers to its capacity to manage your various tasks autonomously, requiring minimal input from you once it's learned your habits and preferences.

Can Lindy manage contracts?

Yes, Lindy can manage contracts. It's one of the multiple tasks it can handle to reduce your workload.

What kind of tasks does Lindy manage?

Lindy manages numerous tasks including email management, scheduling, contract management, note taking, summarizing content, and integrating with other tools amongst other things.

Does Lindy offer any personalization features?

Yes, Lindy offers a high degree of personalization by learning from your habits and preferences. This includes personalized email drafting and prioritizing tasks based on your usage patterns.

What does the daily briefing by Lindy consist of?

The daily briefing by Lindy can include any information it's been programmed to manage, from key emails and tasks to meeting notes and summaries of content.

Can Lindy draft emails on my behalf?

Yes, Lindy can draft emails on your behalf. It will pre-draft replies personalized with your voice for each recipient. You can then review and edit these drafts if necessary before sending.

How does Lindy differentiate between one-off and repeating tasks?

Lindy manages both one-off and repeating tasks. It can learn from your usage patterns to handle repeating tasks automatically, while one-off tasks can be input manually for Lindy to manage.
