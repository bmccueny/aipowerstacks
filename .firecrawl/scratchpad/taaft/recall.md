## Overview

Your personal AI encyclopedia. Turn scattered information into a self-organizing knowledge base that gets smarter as you use it.

Recall is your personal AI encyclopedia. It transforms scattered content into a self-organizing knowledge base that gets smarter the more you use it.

Save content to read later, summarize instantly or chat with your content to cut through the noise and get straight to the point. Take notes, connect ideas, and review what matters on a spaced repetition schedule.

Everything you save is automatically organized and categorized—so it’s easy to find when you need it.

But the most powerful part? Augmented Browsing.

As you explore the web, Recall resurfaces relevant connections in real time, turning passive scrolling into active discovery—all while keeping your data local and secure.

With Recall you can summarize and save the following content:

▶️ YouTube videos

📰 Articles

📃 PDFs

📄 Google Docs

👩‍🍳 Recipes

🎥 Movie and TV series lists

or any other webpages

Key Features of Recall

▶ Save content to read later

▶ Summarize & chat with your content

▶ Customize and link content together

▶ Automatic organization

▶ Automatic knowledge graph

▶ Active recall & spaced repetition

▶ Augmented browsing – resurfaces related content from your knowledge base as you browse

Step 1:

Using our browser extension, summarize and save any online content including YouTube videos, blog posts, news articles and even PDFs.

Step 2:

Your saved summaries are automatically categorized and connected in your knowledge base.

Step 3:

Recall will strategically schedule reviews of your saved summaries to optimize long-term retention. Additionally, Recall will generate questions from the content in your knowledge base to test your knowledge even further
