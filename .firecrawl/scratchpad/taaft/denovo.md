## Overview

Socials: [![linkedin](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/linkedin.svg)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/denovo-dev)[![instagram](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/instagram.svg)](https://www.instagram.com/denovoai/)[![discord](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/discord.svg)](https://discord.com/invite/ztQBFSjeDw)

Denovo is an AI platform that turns raw ideas into fully operational businesses from a single platform. The launchpad guides founders through three core phases -- ideate, build, and operate -- reducing the time, cost, and complexity of launching a startup.

In the ideation phase, founders brainstorm with the platform to refine their concepts. Denovo evaluates market viability, competition, and financial potential, allowing users to validate or pivot before committing significant time or resources.

Once validated, the platform moves into the build phase, automatically generating a business plan, pitch deck, financial model, brand identity, promotional assets, and a functional web application. All components are interconnected, producing a cohesive, end-to-end startup.

When the startup is live, Denovo manages ongoing operations. Founders set goals and allocate budgets while the platform executes across marketing, outreach, and internal workflows. With integrations across more than 1,000 applications -- including Slack, Gmail, GitHub, Instagram, Reddit, and LinkedIn -- it handles product development, content creation, social scheduling, outreach campaigns, and performance tracking.
