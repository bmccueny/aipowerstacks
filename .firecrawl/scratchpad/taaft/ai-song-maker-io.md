## Overview

AI Song Maker is an AI-powered music creation tool that aids in the generation of royalty-free songs. The tool can be used to effortlessly create music tracks using advanced AI technology.

It offers a range of functionalities which simplify the music creation process, making it ideal for users without a detailed musical background. Among the tool's features are 'Lyrics to Song', 'Text to Song', 'Lyrics Generator', 'Extend Song', and 'Vocal Remover'.

Each of these aids in a specific area of song creation, such as transforming text or lyrics into musical compositions, extending song lengths, or even removing vocals from existing tracks.

Additionally, there are a number of AI song generators offered within the platform, including 'Musicgen', 'Riffusion', and 'DiffRhythm', each providing unique capabilities in song generation.

Furthermore, the tool allows for customization of song attributes such as style, genre, tempo, and the type of voice needed. The AI Song Maker also offers functionalities of extending, replacing, and converting sections of the music, making it a versatile tool for music creation.
