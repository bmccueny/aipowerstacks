## Overview

Cora Intelligence is an AI-powered Sales Development Representative (AI SDR) designed to help startups and small teams grow without the cost of hiring a full sales staff.

Instead of spending hours manually researching prospects, sending cold emails, and following up, Cora automates the entire outreach process — from research to initial contact — so founders and sales teams can focus on closing deals.

Here’s how it works:

• AI-Driven Research: Cora automatically gathers and analyzes public business data about potential customers, including their company size, product offerings, pricing strategies, and competitive position.

• Personalized Outreach: It uses this data to craft tailored emails and call scripts that feel genuinely human, leading to higher response rates and more meaningful conversations.

• Multi-Channel Engagement: Cora doesn’t just send emails — it can call leads using natural, human-like AI voices, follow up automatically, and even respond to replies to keep conversations moving.

• Smart Lead Management: All lead data is stored in a centralized, continuously updated database. This creates comprehensive profiles of prospects, helping you track outreach history and understand where each lead is in your pipeline.

• Competitor & Market Insights: Cora identifies if prospects are already working with competitors or selling similar products, giving you actionable intelligence for your pitch.

With Cora, businesses can:

• Consistently generate qualified demos without hiring a team of SDRs.

• Save 20+ hours per week by automating repetitive outreach tasks.

• Reduce customer acquisition costs by focusing on high-quality prospects.

• Gain real-time insights into their market and competition.

Whether you’re a solo founder, a small SaaS team, or a growing startup, Cora provides a scalable, cost-effective way to book more demos, close more deals, and grow revenue — all while spending less time on manual sales work.
