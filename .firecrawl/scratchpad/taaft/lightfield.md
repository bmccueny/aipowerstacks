## Overview

The founders of Lightfield pivoted away from a viral AI presentation tool with 25M+ users to build Lightfield.

Lightfield is an AI-native CRM that captures every conversation from day one and builds your CRM for you in 5 minutes. No fields to define. No pipeline to configure. It reads your emails, meeting transcripts, and calls to build a complete picture of every customer relationship. Ask it to do anything in plain English: draft emails to prospects, build account plans, customer reports and dashboards. Just full customer context, and the work is already done.

It builds a continuous memory of every customer relationship — stitching together emails, meeting transcripts, and notes into a single, searchable history per account. Then it works on that data: updating CRM fields after every meeting, extracting action items, drafting personalized follow-up emails that reference exact words from the conversation, and surfacing deals that have gone quiet.

Lightfield handles meeting prep, post-call summaries, bulk personalized outreach, pipeline updates, dormant deal revival, and cross-team customer context so engineers and product teams can see what customers actually said, not a filtered summary. No configuration required to start. Your data model evolves as your business does.

Ask the agent any question about your business — which objections keep coming up in demos, what your best customers have in common, who requested a feature you just shipped — and get answers grounded in real conversations, with citations.

Built for high-growth teams running 10+ customer conversations a week who've outgrown spreadsheets and want an AI-native CRM that does work for them instead of adding more work.
