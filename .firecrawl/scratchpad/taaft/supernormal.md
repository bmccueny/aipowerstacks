What makes the Supernormal app unique is its focus on turning meetings into completed work. Instead of simply recording conversations, the Supernormal app captures meetings and immediately translates them into finished drafts: tasks, emails, updates, and documents across your tools. For ambitious professionals in product, engineering, sales, and operations, the Supernormal app removes the drag of post-meeting admin and accelerates execution at a consistently high standard.

The Supernormal app is already loved by thousands of early users, and continues to evolve as it expands access to more professionals worldwide.
