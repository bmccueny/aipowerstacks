## Overview

Socials: [![twitter](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/twitter.svg)](https://x.com/mumblenote)[![linkedin](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/linkedin.svg)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/narya-ai/)[![instagram](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/instagram.svg)](https://www.instagram.com/mumblenote/)[![youtube](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/youtube.svg)](https://www.youtube.com/@mumblenote)

Mumble Note is an AI notetaker designed to be the fastest way to capture your scattered thoughts, and turn them into organized notes. You can speak, type or do them both. AI will combine your inputs together and generate structured, actionable and easy-to-retrieve notes, and automatically group notes into different collections based on the content.

Users have been using Mumble Note for meeting notes, quick idea capture, voice journaling, and so much more. It's on iPhone and Apple Watch, with a Mac version coming soon. Perfect for capture and syncing thoughts on the go.

Mumble Note transforms casual, freeform spoken thoughts into structured and functional notes. Users can talk freely to input their meeting notes, ideas, to-dos, or any thoughts, which are instantaneously transformed into organized notes by the AI. The application also allows users to add images, extract text from the image and subsequently take voice notes on them. It provides functionalities beyond transcription, such as turning voice inputs into structured notes that can be easily adjusted or modified. It can generate AI images covers, create to-do lists, ask AI to rewrite or translate notes, any sync tasks with other apps like Apple & Google Calendar, Reminders, Things 3, WhatsApp.

Mumble Note also assists users in organizing and retrieving notes through AI collections. The notes are sorted into AI collections for easy navigation, retrieval, and idea formation. The technology connects scattered notes into organized insights, allowing users to generate creative output. Privacy and security are prioritized, ensuring that all information remains encrypted during transcription.

## Key Features

- Transcribe Your Voice Notes & Meetings
- Generate Ai Summary & Insights
- Identify Tasks In Your Notes And Create To-do List
- Auto-group Voice Notes Into Different Topics
- Integration With Reminder, Calendar, Whatsapp, Google And Things 3
- Generate Use Case Based Output From Voice Notes
- Ai Translation Of Voice Notes
- Identify Frequently Used Words In Speech
- Multi-language Support
- Dark Mode
