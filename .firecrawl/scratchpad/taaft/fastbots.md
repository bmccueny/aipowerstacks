## Overview

Socials: [![facebook](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/facebook.svg)](https://www.facebook.com/people/FastBots/100087080672510/)[![twitter](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/twitter.svg)](https://x.com/fastbotsai/)[![linkedin](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/linkedin.svg)](https://uk.linkedin.com/company/fastbots-ai)[![instagram](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/instagram.svg)](https://www.instagram.com/getfastbots/)[![youtube](https://media.theresanaiforthat.com/assets/youtube.svg)](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCcflXbOhwmaJuOniIhi-ptg)

FastBots is a no-code platform for building AI-powered chatbots trained on your own business data. It’s designed to be simple to use, allowing you to create intelligent assistants that handle customer or internal questions 24/7 using content from your website, PDFs, DOCX and TXT files, and even YouTube videos.

You can fully customise your chatbot to match your brand, from the welcome message and icon to colours and styling, and deploy it anywhere using a simple embed or shareable link. No technical setup required.

FastBots also connects your chatbot beyond your website. You can integrate it with channels like WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, Slack, Email and more, so conversations stay consistent wherever your customers reach out.

Beyond answering questions, FastBots includes AI agents that can carry out tasks, follow multi-step workflows, and help users with more complex requests. When a human touch is needed, the built-in live chat feature lets you or your team step into a conversation in real time, then hand it back to the AI once you’re done. This works especially well for higher-value conversations or support handovers.

Built-in analytics give you visibility into every interaction, helping you understand what users are asking and where your chatbot can improve. Secure, reliable, and quick to deploy, FastBots is a practical way for businesses to automate support, save time, and deliver a smoother customer experience across multiple channels.
