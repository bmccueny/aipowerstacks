1\. Input

Paste a URL, article content, or a simple brief. That's it. No manual editing, no prompt engineering required.

2\. AI Agent

The AI analyzes your content, writes a conversion-optimized script (with hooks and CTAs), generates custom images or video clips, selects animations, and adds a natural-sounding AI voiceover.

3\. Output

You get a ready-to-publish MP4 video optimized for any platform — 1:1 (Instagram/Facebook), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels/Shorts), or 16:9 (YouTube/LinkedIn).
