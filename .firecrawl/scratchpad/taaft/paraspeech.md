## Q&A

What is Paraspeech?

Paraspeech is an ultra-fast, offline speech-to-text tool engineered specifically for macOS systems, primarily designed to enhance efficiency, speed, and privacy. Built in Germany, it adheres strictly to data privacy norms. Users can enjoy faster work processes due to its AI-powered efficient transcription capabilities. A one-time purchase grants lifetime ownership along with 12 months of updates.

How does Paraspeech work?

Paraspeech works by leveraging advanced AI technology to transcribe spoken words into text, all accomplished offline and locally on the user's device. It has been optimized for Apple Silicon to ensure efficient resource utilization. It runs in the background and its operations are triggered only during the transcription process. The tool requires an initial internet connection for model download, after which it operates completely offline.

Is Paraspeech fully offline?

Yes, Paraspeech is fully offline. While it does require an initial internet connection for the model download, once this step is completed, all its transcription operations happen offline, enhancing both speed and data privacy.

What Mac operating system is compatible with Paraspeech?

Paraspeech can function on macOS systems with versions 13.5 and above. It is specifically optimized for devices featuring Apple's M-series chips.

In terms of transcription speed, how does Paraspeech compare to traditional typing?

Paraspeech offers transcription speed that is arguably more than double the speed of traditional typing. For instance, the typical voice input speed using Paraspeech is documented to be around 165 words per minute, while traditional typing speed averages around 75 words per minute.

How does Paraspeech protect user data privacy?

Paraspeech prioritizes user data privacy by ensuring that all transcription activities occur offline and locally on the user's device. This approach prevents any data from leaving the device or getting uploaded or stored on external servers, thereby safeguarding users' voice data and transcriptions.
