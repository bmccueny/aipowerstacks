X-Pilot revolutionizes course creation by turning your documents into professional videos in minutes—no editing skills required. This AI video maker is built exclusively for education, featuring a proprietary Knowledge Visualization Engine with 150+ motion graphics templates that transform abstract concepts into clear, engaging animations.

Simply upload your PDFs, PowerPoints, or Markdown files, and X-Pilot automatically generates structured course videos complete with AI voiceovers, captions, and pedagogical flow.
