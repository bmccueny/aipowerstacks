## Overview

Wordrific is a content creation tool that leverages AI to help B2B content teams produce SEO-optimized articles. Developed with a focus on Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) targeting and aligning with reader needs, Wordrific is aimed at companies seeking consistently high-performing content that stands out in search engine rankings.

The tool utilizes AI capabilities to achieve this task by intelligently researching and crafting content that is designed to rank notably on Google, ChatGPT and Perplexity.

Its features also include autopilot publishing, designed to streamline the content creation and distribution process. This feature could potentially provide companies with the ability to maintain a steady pipeline of SEO-optimized content, reducing the manual involvement and resources spent on the content creation process.

In conclusion, Wordrific exists to aid B2B content teams in the production and distribution of targeted, effective and SEO-optimized written content.
