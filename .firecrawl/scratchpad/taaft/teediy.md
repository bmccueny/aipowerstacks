## Overview

TeeDIY is an online platform that enables users to create custom apparels, particularly T-shirts, instantly and effortlessly. It is designed with the goal of making apparel creation accessible to everybody, regardless of their design skills.

Users can bring any idea to life; they can either upload a photo, type a prompt or start with one of the available templates. The platform provides real-time previews, allowing users to see exactly how their ideas would look on tees or hoodies.

It is also equipped with smart tools such as Magic Cleanup, Style Assist, Auto Layout, and Smart Edit that handle the background removal, style adjustments, spacing and alignment, and precise element changes respectively, making the design process even simpler.

Once users are satisfied with their design, they can place an order. It also offers a curated collection of unique, on-trend apparels for users to shop.

The platform has a wide array of styles and categories to choose from like Men's and Women's T-Shirts of different types, Sweatshirts, Tank Tops, Bottoms, and Pet's apparels.

Moreover, customization can also be done using a variety of templates based on different themes such as Holidays, Collages, Memorials, Fun Pets, ethnic designs, etc.

## Q&A

What is TeeDIY?

TeeDIY is an online design platform which allows users to create personalized apparel. This platform primarily focuses on T-shirts and it strives to make the design process accessible for everyone, regardless of their design abilities.

How does TeeDIY work?

TeeDIY lets users bring any idea to life by uploading a photo, typing a prompt, or starting with a template. It offers real-time previews to show exactly how the designs would appear on clothing. The platform also includes smart tools such as Magic Cleanup, Style Assist, Auto Layout, and Smart Edit for tasks like background removal, style adjustments, alignment and element changes. When users are happy with their design, they can place an order.

What can I design with TeeDIY?

With TeeDIY, users can design a wide array of apparels including men's and women's T-shirts of different types, sweatshirts, tank tops, bottoms, and pet's apparels. User can use their own photos or start with one of the available templates for their designs.

How can I see a real-time preview of my design on TeeDIY?

TeeDIY offers an interface where users can upload their design, make adjustments, and then get an instant, real-time preview of how their idea would look on clothing items such as tees or hoodies.

What smart tools does TeeDIY offer for apparel design?

TeeDIY provides a set of smart tools for apparel design. These include Magic Cleanup for automatic background removal and refining edges, Style Assist for trying different style looks without manual tweaking, Auto Layout for automatic spacing and alignment, and Smart Edit for a precise element change based on the user´s description.

Which apparel options does TeeDIY provide for customization?

TeeDIY provides a wide array of apparel options for customization. These include different styles of men's and women's T-shirts, sweatshirts, tank tops, and bottoms. Pet's apparels are also offered for personalization.

What does the Style Assist feature on TeeDIY do?

The Style Assist feature on TeeDIY helps in customizing the style of the design. It allows users to try different style looks like vintage, comic, sporty, etc., without requiring any manual tweaking.

How do I use the Auto Layout and Smart Edit features on TeeDIY?

The Auto Layout tool on TeeDIY ensures smart spacing and alignment, helping create a balanced design. The Smart Edit tool allows users to precisely change any element in the images; users only need to describe what they want, and TeeDIY achieves it.

Does TeeDIY offer other types of clothes besides T-shirts?

Apart from T-shirts, TeeDIY offers a host of clothing items for personalization. These include multiple styles of sweatshirts, tank tops, and bottoms.

What are the themes available for templates on TeeDIY?

TeeDIY provides templates based on a multitude of themes such as Holidays, Collages, Memorials, Fun Pets, and ethnic designs. This allows users to choose a themed template that resonates best with their idea.

Does TeeDIY offer pet apparels?

How can I order my design on TeeDIY?

After finalizing the design on TeeDIY, users can place an order for it. They only need to pay when they are satisfied with their design and wish to order it.

Are there any costs involved in using TeeDIY?

Can I design without any design skills on TeeDIY?

Absolutely, TeeDIY has been designed in a way that users do not require any design experience. The platform's templates, smart layout, and one-click styles make the design process easy and require only the user's idea.

What happens after I finalize my design on TeeDIY?
