Your chats can access past conversations, files & Agents. Simply click and drag to connect chats to anything in the workspace.

Axell is also the world's first fully multiplayer generative AI tool. Up to 20 people can work inside the same workspace at the same time, with everything synced in real time. Every chat, file, and Agent updates instantly for everyone.
