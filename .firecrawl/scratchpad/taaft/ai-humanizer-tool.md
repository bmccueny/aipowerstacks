## Overview

The AI Humanizer Tool is a web-based AI tool designed to refine output from AI models to make it sound more natural and human-like. Its primary function is to enhance the text generated by AI, particularly in the case of conversational AIs such as GPT-based models, in order to provide more engaging, readable, and relatable content.

It supports a variety of languages including English, Russian, Spanish, Indonesian, Filipino, Hindi, German, French, and Romanian, making it versatile for multilingual AI applications.

It is user-friendly, streamlined for quick text improvements, and it requires no signup, promoting ease of use and accessibility for users across different use-cases.

While it can be particularly beneficial for enhancing AI-driven chatbot interactions, the tool can be applied across a range of text generation domains to improve readability, fluency, and natural language flow of AI-created content.
