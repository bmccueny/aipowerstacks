## Q&A

What is NextDocs?

NextDocs is an AI document generator that creates professional documents such as reports, proposals, resumes, and presentations directly from prompts.

Which types of documents can I create with NextDocs?

You can generate sales proposals, marketing briefs, research reports, pitch decks, and more. Formats include presentations, pageless docs, and traditional documents, with websites coming soon.

How does it work?

Simply input a prompt, like "Generate Sales Proposal for a consulting project", and NextDocs instantly creates a polished, professional draft tailored to your request.

Can I use NextDocs for research-based documents?

Yes, NextDocs can generate structured research reports, complete with clear sections, insights, and a professional tone.

Is NextDocs customizable?

Yes, you can refine prompts and adjust outputs to match your preferred style, tone, or strategic needs, though advanced design customization is limited.

What models does NextDocs use?

NextDocs integrates advanced AI models such as Gemini 2.5 Flash to ensure high-quality, accurate, and professional outputs.
