## Frequently asked questions

Help center

### What is Leadde?

Leadde is dedicated to the intelligent generation of multilingual training videos. With Leadde, you can quickly transform static document into engaging dynamic instructional videos. It is perfect for enhancing engagement in training, marketing, or internal communications.

### Can I create a video with only a voice-over?

Yes, you can. Simply delete or hide the Avatar layer to create a video with voice-over only.

### How to save my video edits?

If you are properly connected to the internet, your video edits will be automatically saved.

### How can I contact Leadde?

If you need help while using Leadde, have any questions about our products or services, or wish to submit feedback or requests, please contact our customer support team by sending an email to support@leadde.ai
