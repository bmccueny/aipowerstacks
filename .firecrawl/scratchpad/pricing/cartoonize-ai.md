## Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to us

### What happens when I run out of credits?

If you have a subscription, your credit quota will automatically reset at the start of each billing cycle. If you need more credits before the reset, you can purchase additional credit packs for immediate use.

### How long are my credits valid?

Subscription credits reset monthly on your billing date. Credits from credit packs have the same validity period as your subscription and will expire on your next billing date. We recommend using them before renewal.

### Can I get a refund for unused credits?

For subscriptions, you can cancel anytime but we don't provide refunds for past usage. For credit packs, due to the immediate allocation of computational resources, we cannot offer refunds. Please carefully consider your needs before purchasing.

### If I am not satisfied with the results, can I get a refund?

We do not offer refunds for our services. However, we are always happy to help you with any issues you may encounter. Please contact our support team for assistance.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription in the Billing & Subscription section of your account.

### How is NSFW or Adult Content handled?

The creation of any form of NSFW or adult content is strictly prohibited across all plans. We reserve the right to delete such content and take appropriate action if discovered.

### What if my preferred payment method isn't supported?

Please contact our support team, and we'll work with you to find an alternative payment solution that meets your needs.

### What does commercial usage include?

All paid plans (both subscriptions and credit packs) include commercial usage rights, allowing you to use generated results for business purposes. The Free plan does not include commercial usage rights.
