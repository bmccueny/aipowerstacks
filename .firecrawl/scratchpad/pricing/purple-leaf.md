## Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

You pay per tool use. No subscriptions or monthly fees—just pay for what you need, when you need it. Each tool shows its price upfront, and you only pay when you use it.

What are Purple Leaf Tokens?

Tokens are prepaid credits where 1 token = $1. When you buy tokens in bundles, you save up to 20% compared to paying per tool. Tokens can be used across any tool and never expire.

Can I try a tool before buying?

Most tools show previews or sample outputs so you can see what you'll get. If a tool fails to process your request, you automatically receive a full refund or credit.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

If a tool doesn't work as expected or the output isn't useful, contact us within 24 hours and we'll issue a refund or credit to your account.

Do tokens expire?

No. Tokens never expire and can be used across any tool at any time. Use them at your own pace.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We use Stripe for secure payment processing and never store your credit card details. All transactions are encrypted and PCI-compliant.
