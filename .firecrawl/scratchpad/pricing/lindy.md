## Frequently asked questions.

[**How do I start using Lindy?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Getting started takes about 60 seconds. Sign up for a free 7-day trial, connect your phone number, and you'll receive a text from Lindy.

[**Does Lindy work on Android?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Yes! Lindy works with any SMS-capable device. While our interface is optimized for iMessage on iOS, Android users can text Lindy and receive responses via standard SMS.

[**What's included in the free trial?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

The 7-day free trial gives you full access to all Plus features including inbox management, meeting scheduling and follow-up, meeting recording and notes, and the ability to text your AI assistant 24/7.

[**Can I cancel anytime?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts on the Plus plan. You can cancel from your account settings at any time, and you'll retain access through the end of your billing period.

[**How does Lindy learn my style?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Lindy observes how you write emails, respond to messages, and handle your calendar over time. The more you use it, the better it gets at drafting replies that sound like you and prioritizing what matters most.

[**Will Lindy send messages without my approval?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

No. Lindy drafts emails and messages for you to review. You're always in control of what gets sent.

[**What integrations does Lindy support?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Lindy connects with hundreds of apps including Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, and more. Enterprise customers can request custom integrations as part of their onboarding.

[**Is my data secure?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Yes. Lindy uses enterprise-grade encryption and never shares your data with third parties. Enterprise plans include additional security features like SSO, SCIM provisioning, and audit logs.

[**What's the difference between Plus and Enterprise?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Plus is designed for individuals who want a powerful AI assistant. Enterprise is built for teams and organizations that need more robust controls, visibility and security. Contact our sales team for Enterprise pricing.

[**What if I need help getting set up?**\\
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63e15df811f9df22b231e58f/6734a4c75fe417dce9d7b85a_5e37b729a3d85b636a279df8_plus.svg.svg)](https://www.lindy.ai/pricing#)

Plus users can access our help center and community support. Enterprise customers receive dedicated onboarding, enablement sessions, and a support team to ensure your whole organization gets value from Lindy.
