## Frequently Asked Questions

for more info

### What's the difference between Free, Pro, and Max?

- Free Plan: $0 / month
  - Credits: 15 per day / 25 per month
  - Highlights: Forever free, best for light use
- Pro Plan: Starting from $20 / month
  - Credits: 15 per day / 25 per month
  - Flexible tiers (per month): 100 → $20 (default), 250 → $50, 350 → $70
  - Highlights: Designed for professionals with daily high-frequency usage
- Max Plan: Starting from $100 / month
  - Credits: 15 per day / 25 per month
  - Flexible tiers (per month): 500 → $100(default), 1000 → $200, 1500 → $300 , up to 10000
  - Highlights: Higher accuracy, power and storage. Race mode improves accuracy up to 3×

For more details, please visit our [Help center](https://help.atoms.dev/en/collections/15118226-plans-billing).

### Can I switch plans at any time? When will the change take effect?

Yes, you can switch anytime.

- Upgrade: Takes effect immediately after payment, but the billing cycle will not reset. It continues based on your original cycle. Upgrading only requires paying the price difference.

_Example: You purchased a $20 plan and have used it for 10 days (20 days remaining in the cycle). If you upgrade to the $100 plan now, the new plan will be active for the remaining 20 days; any unused credits will roll over to the next month (rollover is only for one month, not permanent)._
- Downgrade: Takes effect on the first day after your current billing cycle ends. If storage capacity decreases, the system will remind you in advance to export or clear projects to avoid any impact on your Atoms usage. Downgrades are non-refundable and must wait until the current billing cycle ends.

_Example: If you purchased a $100 plan and have used it for 10 days (20 days remaining in the cycle), downgrading to a $20 plan will take effect only after the current cycle ends (20 days later). The $100 plan will expire, and the $20 plan will start the next day. Any unused Credits will roll over to the next month (rollover is only for one month, not permanent)._

### Can I buy extra credits if I run out?

You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time on the Settings → Plans and credits page or the Pricing → Payment page to get more Credits, which will take effect immediately.

### Will my unused Credits be reset to zero?

Depending on the specific situation, unused Credits will be automatically carried over to the next billing cycle in the following cases _(rollover is only for one month, not permanent):_

- Unused Credits after an upgrade
- Extra bonus Credits granted by the system in addition to the daily fixed Credits (15) under special circumstances
- Credits earned from inviting new users
- Credits obtained through redemption codes

### Will downgrading / upgrading affect my data?

If downgrading / upgrading reduces your storage limit, you'll get a reminder to back up or delete files to prevent any impact on the normal use of Atoms.

### Are subscriptions auto-renewed?

Yes. Both Pro and Max plans renew automatically every month unless you downgrade via the Settings → Plans and credits page or the Pricing → Payment page before your next billing cycle starts.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can downgrade your current plan to the Free plan at any time via the Settings → Plans and credits page or the Pricing → Payment page. The downgrade will take effect on the first day after the current billing cycle ends.

### Where can I check my plan and billing info?

You can view your current plan, Credits tier, remaining balance, and billing history anytime in the Settings → Plans and credits page or the Pricing → Payment page.

### What are credits and how are they used?

- Credits are points used to access services, including daily bonus credits, monthly subscription credits, and reward credits.
- The system automatically uses credits in this order: daily credits (15 per day) first, then subscription credits, and finally bonus credits.
