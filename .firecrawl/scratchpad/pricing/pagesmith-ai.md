## Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any differences.

Is the free version permanently free?

Yes, the free plan is permanently free. You can use it as long as you want with no time limits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Billing is monthly or annually.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no commitment. Your sites remain accessible until the end of the billing period.

Do I own the code I export?

Yes, 100%. When you export your site, you get a standard Astro repository that you own completely. Host it anywhere, modify it freely — it's your intellectual property.

What happens to my sites if I downgrade?

Your existing sites remain published but you won't be able to publish new ones beyond your plan limit. You can always export your code before downgrading.
