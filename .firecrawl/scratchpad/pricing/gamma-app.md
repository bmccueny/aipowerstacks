## Frequently asked questions

What AI models does Gamma use?

We leverage a variety of the most advanced AI models to provide you with the best possible experience. These include:

- Claude (Anthropic)
- ChatGPT + DALL-E (OpenAI)
- Gemini + Imagen (Google)
- Flux
- Recraft
- Playground
- Ideogram
- Luma
- Leonardo

This combination allows us to offer powerful capabilities in text generation, image creation, and design assistance while ensuring your data privacy and security.

What are AI credits?

AI credits measure advanced AI usage. On the Free plan, all actions use credits. On paid plans, presentations and images with standard models are included. Credits are only used for:

- Agent
- Premium AI models
- API access

How can I get more AI credits?

Paid plans include monthly credits:

- Plus: 1,000 / month
- Pro: 4,000 / month
- Ultra: 20,000 / month
- Team: 6,000 / seat / month
- Business: 10,000 / seat / month

Unused credits roll over up to 2× the monthly amount. Example: Plus can hold up to 2,000.

Paid users can purchase additional credits.

Free plan includes 400 starter credits that do not refill. Free plan users can invite friends and earn credits when they join.

What is a card?

A card represents one slide of a presentation, or one section of a webpage or document. Unlike traditional slides, cards can expand to fit their content.

A card can hold text, images, videos, or other media. Cards can be easily rearranged, styled, and transformed, allowing you to create dynamic presentations, documents, or websites.

Can I use Gamma with PowerPoint?

Yes! You can export any content you create as a PowerPoint (PPT) or PDF file. Alternatively, you can also import any PPT file into Gamma to be enriched.

Is there a discount on annual billing?

Yes! You can save up to 28% by choosing an annual plan compared to monthly billing.

Can I change my plan or cancel at any time?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from "Settings & members". If you cancel, you'll retain access to your paid features and credits until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account automatically transitions to the Free plan. You can learn more about how to manage your plan [here](https://help.gamma.app/en/articles/8568920-how-to-manage-or-cancel-your-gamma-subscription).

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit card brands. You can also pay directly via any US or EU bank. For customers in India, UPI payments are supported.

How do I get charged when adding a member to my workspace?

On individual paid plans (Plus, Pro, Ultra), you are charged for each additional seat when a member accepts their invitation.

On Team and Business plans, seats are purchased upfront. You can invite members up to the number of seats you’ve purchased. If you need more seats, you can buy them at any time.

What happens to my content if I downgrade or cancel my subscription?

Your content remains yours, regardless of your subscription status. If you downgrade or cancel, you'll retain access to all your created content, but some advanced features may be limited.

Can I create my own company-branded theme in Gamma?

Yes, you can easily create themes inside our theme builder.

What is the public API?

Gamma offers an API that lets you connect Gamma to other tools and workflows. It can be used directly by developers or through no-code platforms like Zapier and Make. Access is available on paid plans and uses AI credits. You can get an overview [here](https://gamma.app/products/api) and read our documentation [here](https://developers.gamma.app/).
