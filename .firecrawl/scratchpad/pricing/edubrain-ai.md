### Frequently asked questions

Is EduBrain free?

Yes, the Free plan costs nothing. You get unlimited attempts per homework tool, file upload, and downloads. You can try slides, flashcards, and answers without payment. Later, move to AI-Plus if you want to experience advanced reasoning with no ads.

What do I get with AI-Plus?

The paid plan clears the ads and unlocks advanced reasoning for your tasks. It also gives you access to human support at discounted prices.

Any hidden limits on “unlimited”?

“Unlimited” means you can work as much as you need. If a limit applies, the app shows it in advance and we inform you about updates.

Can I trust the answers?

Good output starts with a clear prompt. Ask for step-by-step and cite-ready notes. Recheck facts in sensitive tasks. The tool speeds you up; you keep control.

Is this safe for academic integrity?

Use it as a study aid. Drafts, steps, and sources guide your work. You stay the author. Follow your course policy at all times. When in doubt, keep a citation.

Will I get a refund?

Refunds depend on what happened. Wrong charge or a clear error gets priority. Send the order ID and date. We review quickly and respond with the outcome.

What about privacy and data use?

Privacy comes first. Your files stay private unless you share them. The [policy](https://edubrain.ai/privacy-policy/) explains everything.

Will teachers detect my work as AI?

No tool can guarantee that result. Run the solution through the [AI humaniser](https://edubrain.ai/ai-humanizer/) to match your voice, review it, add your sources, then [check the text with the AI detector](https://edubrain.ai/ai-detector/) and adjust again if necessary.

What happens if I hit a limit?

The app shows possible limits in advance. You can change tools or upgrade when you want and your work never cuts off without warning.

Can I share my results?

Yes, each output has a share link.
