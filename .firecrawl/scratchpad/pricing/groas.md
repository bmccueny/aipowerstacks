## FAQ

Can I configure rules or limits for groas to follow?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

No. When we say fully autonomous, we mean it. groas makes every decision without waiting for your approval - that's the point. You get full visibility through weekly emails and slack updates, not a dashboard of buttons to second-guess it.

Can I use groas to optimize only a subset of my Google Ads search campaigns while keeping others manual?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

You can connect groas to as many or as few campaigns as you'd like in order to get started. Generally after a couple weeks of getting comfortable with the product, most customers end up moving all of their campaigns to groas.

How does groas work with my current website / landing pages?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

We integrate with any type of website or landing page software you have. Getting setup only requires adding one line of JavaScript onto your website and then you're ready to go.

What happens to the campaigns I connected groas with if I decide to cancel down the line?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

They can keep running as normal just without any of the groas optimisations still in place.

Do you offer discounts or free trials?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

No. If less than 5% of ad spend with no lock-ins feels like too much for fully autonomous management that outperforms humans + dynamic landers + a dedicated PPC account manager then we'd rather not take your business.

What industries does groas work best for?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6821efca072e48f6f495a47e/6825d58acb53bb89d1d3fa2c_Plus%20Icon.svg)

Pretty much all of them. We haven't yet seen one where groas didn't work as expected. If you're running any form of Google Search Ads, then groas is here to make you more money.
