## FAQ

How do credits work?

Credits are used for each AI action (script generation, images, voice, editing). A standard video consumes on average 85 credits (slideshow format). Your credits never expire.

Is there a monthly subscription?

No, Fliz operates solely on a pay-per-use model. You buy credit packs when you need them, without any commitment or recurring fees.

Can I use Fliz via API?

Yes! Fliz offers a complete REST API to automate your video production. Your pack credits are usable indifferently via the web interface, via the API or via automation services like N8N, Make, Zapier or our WordPress plugin.

What formats are supported?

Fliz can generate videos in 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square) and 16:9 (horizontal) formats, adapting to all platforms: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube or LinkedIn.
