## Frequently Asked Questions

What subscription plans are available in iMini AI?

iMini AI offers four plans: a Free Plan with limited features, the Pro Plan with 10,000 credits, the Max Plan with 30,000 credits, and the Ultra Plan with 100,000 credits.

How do credits work?

Each tool requires a set number of credits. You can view the cost in the table above, and the exact credits needed for your task will be displayed before you hit “Generate.”

How can I get more credits if I run out?

You can upgrade to a higher-tier plan at any time or purchase one-off credit packs whenever you need more.

What if I decide to upgrade monthly subscription to year subscription?

If you switch from a monthly plan to a yearly plan, your remaining monthly subscription value will be recalculated and applied to your new yearly plan.

What payment options are available in iMini AI?

iMini AI accepts multiple payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfer, Amazon pay, Klarna, and Alipay.

Do unused monthly credits roll over to the next billing cycle?

No. Monthly-plan credits refresh on your billing date and any unused credits will be cleared.

What if I decide to cancel?

You can cancel your subscription at any time if you no longer wish to use iMini AI.

How can I monitor my remaining credits?

You can view your remaining credits directly in your profile dashboard.

How do I contact iMini AI support team?

You can directly email support@imini.com if you have any questions to get more support.
