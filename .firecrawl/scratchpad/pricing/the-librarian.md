## Frequently asked questions

##### Who would benefit most from using The Librarian?

Any busy professionals on Google Workspace who can benefit from efficient task management. Designed for those who have busy schedules and require timely insights from their work Apps to make informed decisions.

##### Can I try The Librarian for free?

Yes, we are currently offering the Basic version of The Librarian for free. Soon we will be launching a Premium version with some paid Advanced features. See our [pricing page](https://thelibrarian.io/pricing.html) for details.

##### How secure is my data with The Librarian?

We take security seriously and use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data. Your privacy is our top priority, and we don’t share your information with third parties.

##### Will The Librarian access information I don’t have access to?

No, The Librarian only accesses data that you have permission to view. If you don’t have access to a document, channel, or page in a connected app, The Librarian won’t be able to retrieve it for you.

##### How can I reach support if I need help?

Our support team is here to assist you! For inquiries, reach out via email at [support@thelibrarian.io](mailto:support@thelibrarian.io). We’re committed to providing timely support to ensure a seamless experience with The Librarian.
