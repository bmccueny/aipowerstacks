### What is Outfit?

Outfit is a platform that allows you to create high-quality on-model photos for your fashion brand.

### How does it work?

Take a photo of your clothing item, choose a model and customize your shoot. Within seconds, you'll get stunning photos of your product on a model, ready to use anywhere.

### Is it free to try?

Yes! You can try Outfit for free with a limited number of credits. Simply sign up and start creating photos.

### How much does it cost?

Our pricing is simple and flexible - just pay for what you need, with no recurring subscriptions. Each generated photo only costs 1 credit and each video costs 10 credits.

### Will I lose my credits if I don't use them?

No, once you purchase credits, they are yours to use whenever you want to. Your credits never expire!

### Can I use the photos commercially?

Absolutely! You have full rights to use all the photos you generate commercially. Whether for your website, ads, social media, or any other commercial purposes, feel free to utilize them as needed.

### Are my photos private?

Yes, all the photos you upload and generate using Outfit are completely private. Your images are securely stored and accessible only to you.

### Do you offer refunds?

Due to the high costs associated with AI inference and processing, we are unable to offer refunds on credits once purchased.

Should you encounter any issues, wish to provide feedback, or simply want to get in touch, feel free to send an email to [yo@outfit.fm](mailto:yo@outfit.fm).
