## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

### Do unused credits roll over?

Credits reset each billing cycle and don't roll over. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your monthly usage.

### Is there a free trial?

All paid plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start with our Free plan.

### Can I buy additional credits?

Yes! You can purchase additional credit packs anytime if you need more than your monthly allocation.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Business plans. All payments are processed securely.

### Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
