## Common Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your dashboard. If you upgrade, the change is instant. If you downgrade, it takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.

### What happens to my site if I stop paying?

Your site will be unpublished and saved in your account for 30 days. You can reactivate it anytime by renewing your subscription.

### Is hosting included?

Yes! All plans come with premium, secure cloud hosting. You don't need to pay extra for hosting services.

### Can I use my own domain?

Absolutely. On the Professional plan and above, you can connect any custom domain (e.g., yourbusiness.com) in just a few clicks.
