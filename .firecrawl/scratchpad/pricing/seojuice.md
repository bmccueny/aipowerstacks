## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about plans and pricing

### What happens when I decide to leave?

When you cancel, you can export all your SEO data — internal links, meta descriptions, alt texts, and more. To keep live optimizations active on your site for free, simply add a small backlink to seojuice.com. Otherwise, you can implement the exported data manually in your CMS.


### Do you have a lower/free option?

Yes! Add a small backlink to seojuice.com and get 100 pages optimized for free with weekly indexing — no credit card required. You also get 1 monitored competitor and 1 AI search monitoring prompt. Paid plans include daily crawling with priority processing, plus premium features like Google Business Profile optimization and unlimited competitor monitoring.


### Can I add more than 50 sites or more than 50,000 pages?

We have no limit to how many sites (dashboards) or pages you can have in your SEOJuice account. 50 sites are the default that the highest plan come with, meaning you can add up to 50 separate sites to your SEOJuice account without incurring extra fees. If you want to add more than 50 sites to your account, contact us.


### How is tax handled?

All prices shown are in EUR excluding tax. At checkout, applicable taxes (such as VAT) are automatically calculated based on your location and added to the total. The displayed prices are also automatically converted to your local currency at checkout using current exchange rates.


### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. You won't be charged after cancellation.


### Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. The changes will be reflected immediately.


### Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes, for larger organizations or agencies managing multiple high-traffic websites, we offer custom enterprise plans with additional features like priority support, custom integrations, and flexible billing options. Contact us to discuss your needs.


### Do you offer refunds?

Since we offer a generous free plan, we do not provide refunds on paid subscriptions. You can try all core features for free before upgrading, and you can cancel at any time with no questions asked.


### What if I have another question?

Reach out to us via the live chat feature on the bottom right corner of the page. We will be happy to help with any pre-sales questions.
