## AI Song Maker Pricing FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About AI Song Maker Pricing Plans.

- ### Is it safe to purchase a plan on AI Song Maker?






Yes, it is safe to purchase a plan on AI Song Maker. We use secure payment gateways to process transactions and ensure that your payment information is protected. Your privacy and security are our top priorities, and we take all necessary precautions to safeguard your data.

- ### How do music credits work?






Music credits are used to generate songs and use features in AI Song Maker. Annual subscribers receive all yearly credits upfront, while monthly subscribers receive credits every month. If you run out of credits, you can either re-subscribe at https://www.aisongmaker.io/pricing (the new subscription replaces the old one and credits are added), or buy one-time credit packs at https://www.aisongmaker.io/my-subscription. This ensures you can keep creating music anytime.

- ### Personal and Commercial Use






Free users can publish their songs on platforms like YouTube or Spotify, but must credit AI Song Maker: https://www.aisongmaker.io/. Subscribers enjoy full commercial rights for all songs they create. Songs generated in the 7 days before subscribing will automatically receive commercial rights once you subscribe. If you cancel your subscription, commercial rights for previously created songs remain valid.

- ### Can I get commercial rights for past free songs after subscribing?






Yes. Songs generated within the 7 days before your subscription starts will automatically receive commercial rights.

- ### Do my songs keep commercial rights after I cancel?






Yes. Songs generated during your active subscription period will continue to have commercial rights even after you cancel your subscription.

- ### Do unused credits roll over?






Yes. Any unused credits will be carried over and added to your balance when your subscription renews.

- ### Will my credits remain after cancellation?






Yes. Your credits will remain in your account after cancellation, and any new credits from a future subscription will be added to your existing balance.

- ### How do I cancel my subscription?






You can cancel your subscription by going to https://www.aisongmaker.io/my-subscription to manage your billing details. After cancellation, all songs you previously generated will still retain commercial rights.

- ### How can I change my plan?






If you want to upgrade or downgrade your subscription, you can go to https://www.aisongmaker.io/my-subscription to manage your billing details. In this case, you only need to pay the price difference, and the change will take effect in the next billing cycle. If you want the change to take effect immediately, please visit https://www.aisongmaker.io/pricing and subscribe to your desired plan directly. We will cancel your previous plan and add the remaining credits to your new subscription.

- ### Can I resubscribe to a plan?






Yes! You can resubscribe anytime at https://www.aisongmaker.io/pricing. Your new subscription will replace the previous one, and any remaining credits will be added. If your subscription is about to expire and you only need a small number of credits, you can also purchase a temporary credit pack at https://www.aisongmaker.io/my-subscription.

- ### Can I pay with PayPal?






Yes. You can choose your preferred plan using the PayPal subscription links below.
