## Frequently Asked Questions about AI Music Generation

Everything you need to know about creating 8-minute, royalty-free music with built-in commercial rights.

### What is an AI Music Generator?

It turns text prompts or lyrics into complete songs. Our AI creates original tracks up to 8 minutes in any genre or mood, in under a minute, ready for commercial use.

### How does it work?

Enter a short idea, mood, or full lyrics. The AI composes melodies, harmonies, and instrumentals, then renders high-quality audio you can download within seconds.

### Is it free to try?

Yes. New users get free credits to generate songs, lyrics, and instrumentals. Upgrade for longer generations, higher-quality audio, and full commercial rights.

### Can I use the music commercially?

Yes. Every download includes a commercial license—safe for Spotify, YouTube, TikTok, ads, films, games, and client projects, with proof of rights.

### How long can songs be?

Up to 8 minutes per track—far beyond typical 2–4 minute limits—great for long-form content, podcasts, and ambient use.

### Can I add vocals or keep it instrumental?

You can generate with or without vocals. You can also remove vocals or split stems later for remixes or karaoke.

### Can I customize genre, mood, or instruments?

Yes. Choose genre, mood, tempo, instruments, and structure. AI adapts to your input to match your sound.

### What formats can I download?

Export WAV or MP3. Pro plans unlock higher bitrate/quality suitable for release.

### Who owns the copyright of the generated music?

You own the rights to tracks you generate under a paid plan, with a license included for verification.

### Is AI-generated music allowed on Spotify/YouTube?

Yes. Platforms focus on listener engagement, not how music is made. Your licensed tracks can be uploaded and recommended like any other.
