## Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how Wellows helps you monitor AI visibility, measure performance, and choose the plan that fits your growth. Still have questions? [Book a Demo](https://wellows.com/book-a-demo/)

What's the difference between the Lite, Essential, Starter, and Pro plans?

The Lite plan is designed for solo agencies and marketers who want focused, affordable AI visibility tracking for a single domain. It includes core monitoring, content insights, outreach opportunities, and strategic guidance, making it ideal for getting started without complexity.

The Essential plan builds on Lite by expanding core visibility-tracking features for growing teams that need broader coverage, more tracking capacity, and deeper insights.

The Starter plan further extends platform coverage, regional tracking, search prompts, and content generation limits, making it suitable for scaling teams and multi-site operations.

The Pro plan offers the highest usage limits and full-scale AI search monitoring capabilities, built for enterprises and large teams that require advanced analytics, wide platform coverage, and large-volume tracking.

Which AI platforms does each plan support?

The Lite plan supports monitoring on OpenAI only, making it ideal for focused, single-domain AI visibility tracking.

The Essential plan supports monitoring across OpenAI and Google AI Overviews, providing broader foundational AI search coverage.

The Starter plan includes coverage across OpenAI, Gemini, Perplexity, AI Overviews, and AI Mode, enabling multi-platform AI visibility tracking and discovery.

The Pro plan also monitors OpenAI, Gemini, Perplexity, AI Overviews, and AI Mode, offering the same platform coverage as the Starter plan but with significantly higher usage limits, deeper analytics, and enterprise-scale monitoring capacity.

How many competitors can I track per plan?

All three plans—Essential, Starter, and Pro—allow you to track up to ten competitors, ensuring consistent competitive insight regardless of the plan you choose.

How many search prompts are included with each plan?

The Lite plan includes 40 monthly prompts for focused, single-domain AI visibility tracking on OpenAI, making it ideal for solo agencies and marketers getting started.

The Essential plan includes one hundred monthly search prompts, the Starter plan increases that amount to four hundred prompts, and the Pro plan offers one thousand prompts each month to support higher-volume visibility tracking and large-scale AI search monitoring.

How many regions can I monitor?

The Lite plan allows monitoring across one region, making it ideal for focused, single-market visibility tracking.

The Essential plan allows monitoring across two regions, while both the Starter and Pro plans allow monitoring across five regions, making them better suited for teams operating in multiple markets and regions.

Does Wellows integrate with Google Search Console?

Yes, every plan includes Google Search Console integration so you can connect your SEO data with your AI search visibility insights.

How much content can Wellows generate per month?

The Lite plan allows users to generate two pieces of content per month, making it ideal for light, focused content needs.

The Essential plan includes the ability to generate five pieces of content per month. The Starter plan increases this limit to fifteen pieces per month, while the Pro plan supports up to seventy content pieces each month for teams that require significant content output.

Are implicit and explicit citation checks limited?

No. All plans include unlimited tracking for both implicit and explicit citations, giving you complete insight into how AI models reference your brand across platforms.

Are verified outreach emails included?

Yes. Verified outreach emails are unlimited on every plan, allowing your team to manage citation corrections and outreach without restrictions.

How often does Wellows monitor AI search results?

Wellows performs monitoring daily across all plans, ensuring that your team receives up-to-date visibility data.

Is historical data included?

Yes. Every plan includes full access to historical data, allowing you to compare performance over time and measure long-term trends.

Do all plans include a free trial?

Yes. Each plan includes a seven-day free trial so you can fully explore the platform before committing.
