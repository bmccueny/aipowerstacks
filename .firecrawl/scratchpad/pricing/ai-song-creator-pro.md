## Payment & Subscription FAQs

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment options, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.

### Do unused credits roll over to the next billing cycle?

No, unused credits expire at the end of each billing cycle. Please use your credits within the current month to get the most out of your plan.

### What if I need more credits?

If you run out of credits, you can upgrade your plan anytime to unlock higher limits and additional features.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden charges. The price shown on the plan page is exactly what you pay—no extra fees or surprise costs.

### What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available under specific conditions outlined in our Refund Policy. You can also cancel your subscription anytime to stop future billing.
