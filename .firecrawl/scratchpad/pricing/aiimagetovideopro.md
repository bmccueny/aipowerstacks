### What are "Credits" and how do they work for both free and paid plans?

Credits are the currency used to generate videos on our platform. Each output consumes a specific number of credits. For paid plans (Standard and Pro), credits renew monthly or yearly, depending on your subscription.

### Does unused credit roll over to the next month or year?

No. Monthly credits on the Standard and Pro plans expire at the end of your billing cycle. Unused credits from annual subscriptions expire at the end of the yearly term. Enterprise plans may be eligible for custom rollover agreements.

### What happens if I run out of credits during the month?

If you reach your monthly credit limit, your ability to generate new content will be paused until your next billing cycle. You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time to immediately receive a new credit allocation.

### What is the difference between the Standard, Pro, and Enterprise plans?

The Standard plan is our entry-level paid option. The Pro plan is recommended for frequent users and small teams, offering a significantly higher credit limit and Priority Customer Support. The Enterprise plan is a customized solution for large organizations, including custom credit pricing, dedicated support, and advanced security features.

### Do you offer a yearly discount?

Yes. When you choose to pay yearly, you receive a significant discount compared to the monthly subscription rate. The annual payment is billed as a single lump sum for the entire year.

### Can I upgrade or cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. You can manage all changes via your account settings page. Upgrades take effect immediately. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and your paid access will remain active until the end of the current billing period (monthly or yearly).
