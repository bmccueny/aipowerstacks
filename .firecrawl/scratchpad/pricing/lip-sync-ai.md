## Lip Sync AI Pricing FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Lip Sync AI Pricing Plans.

- ### Is it safe to purchase a plan on Lip Sync AI?






Yes, it is safe to purchase a plan on Lip Sync AI. We use secure payment gateways to process transactions and ensure that your payment information is protected. Your privacy and security are our top priorities, and we take all necessary precautions to safeguard your data.

- ### How do video credits work?






Video credits are used to generate videos with Lip Sync AI. Each plan comes with a set number of video credits that can be used to create videos. Once you run out of video credits, you can purchase additional credits or upgrade to a higher plan to continue using the service.

- ### Can I change my plan?






Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you need more video credits or want access to additional features, you can upgrade to a higher plan. If you find that you no longer need certain features, you can downgrade to a lower plan. Your plan changes will take effect immediately, and you will be billed accordingly.

- ### What if I decide to cancel my subscription?






If you decide to cancel your subscription, you can do so at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing cycle, after which you will no longer be charged. You can still access your account and use any remaining video credits until the end of the billing cycle.
