## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating copyright-safe, royalty-free music with commercial license included.

### What is Create Music AI?

Create Music AI is an AI music generator that turns text or lyrics into complete songs. It creates original tracks up to 8 minutes in any genre or mood in under 60 seconds, 100% royalty-free with commercial license included.

### Is it really free to use?

Yes. New users get 1 music generation (2 songs) per month free to try all features. Upgrade for higher volume, unlimited downloads, and priority support starting at $14.99/month.

### Can I use the music commercially on YouTube, Spotify, TikTok?

Absolutely. Every track includes a full commercial license for YouTube monetization, Spotify releases, TikTok ads, podcasts, films, games, and client work. Each download includes a license certificate for verification. You keep 100% of your revenue.

### How fast can I create music?

From idea to download in under 60 seconds. Enter a prompt or lyrics, click generate, and get studio-quality audio instantly—no rendering queues, no waiting.

### Who owns the copyright of the generated music?

You own the rights to all tracks you generate under a paid plan. Each download includes a license certificate for verification and proof of commercial rights.

### Is AI-generated music allowed on streaming platforms?

Yes. Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, and other platforms allow AI-generated music. They focus on listener engagement, not how music is made. Your licensed tracks can be uploaded, monetized, and recommended like any other song.

### Can I add vocals or create instrumental-only tracks?

Yes. Generate songs with vocals, instrumental-only, or both. You can also use our vocal remover and stem splitter tools to extract or isolate specific parts later.

### Can I customize genre, mood, tempo, and instruments?

Absolutely. Choose from dozens of genres (pop, rock, hip-hop, jazz, classical, etc.), set the mood (uplifting, dark, romantic), adjust tempo, and specify instruments. The AI adapts to your creative vision.

### What audio formats can I download?

Download in WAV or MP3 format. All plans include high-quality audio downloads. Paid plans unlock higher bitrates suitable for professional releases.

### How long can the generated songs be?

Up to 8 minutes per track—far longer than typical 2-4 minute AI music generators. Perfect for full-length releases, podcasts, long-form videos, and ambient background music.
