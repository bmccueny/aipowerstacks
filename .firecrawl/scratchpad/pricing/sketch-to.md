### Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing plans

#### Do unused credits roll over?

To ensure we can offer the most competitive pricing and generous credit allowances each month, your credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. This model allows us to provide you with the best possible value in every billing period.

#### Can I use the generated images commercially?

Yes, commercial use is a key feature of our Pro plan. Pro subscribers own the rights to their creations and can use them for any commercial purpose, including marketing, advertising, and resale. Images generated under the Basic plan are licensed for personal use only.

#### What happens if I run out of credits?

Don't let your creativity stop! You have a few great options: (1) Purchase a Credit Pack for an instant one-time top-up — no subscription needed. (2) Upgrade your plan to get more credits and unlock more features instantly. (3) Wait for your next billing cycle when credits will automatically refresh. We'll send you a notification when you're running low so you can decide what's best for you.

#### What are Credit Packs?

Credit Packs are one-time purchases that give you credits instantly without a subscription. They do not auto-renew, so you only pay when you need more. Purchased credits are valid for 1 year from the date of purchase. You can buy multiple packs at any time to top up your balance.

#### How do I cancel my subscription?

We believe in flexibility. You can cancel your subscription at any time with just a few clicks in your account settings. No questions asked. After canceling, you'll continue to have access to your plan's features and credits until the end of your current billing period.

#### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through Stripe. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

#### What counts as one credit?

Typically, generating one image uses 2 credits. However, certain advanced features or higher resolutions might consume more. You will always see the credit cost before you generate an image, so there are no surprises.

#### Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! Every new user gets 4 free credits upon signing up to experience the power of our AI. No credit card required. If you love it and need more, you can subscribe to one of our plans anytime.

#### What's the difference between Basic and Pro plans?

Basic plan is perfect for personal use and casual creators, offering 200 credits (~100 images) per month. Pro plan is designed for power users and professionals, with commercial license, priority support, and 800 credits (~400 images) — that's 4x more images for just $10 more!
