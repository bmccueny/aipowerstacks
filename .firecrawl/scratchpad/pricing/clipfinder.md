## Questions?

### How does the free tier work?

Every month, you get 2 hours of video processing for free. This resets on the 1st of each month. Perfect for occasional use.

### What counts as an "hour"?

We charge based on the source video duration, not processing time. A 3-hour stream = 3 hours billed, regardless of how many clips you extract.

### Are there any hidden fees?

None. No subscriptions, no setup fees, no per-clip charges. Just pay for the video hours you process beyond the free tier.
