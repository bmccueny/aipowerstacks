## Frequently Asked Questions

1\. What is a credit, and how is it calculated?

In Math AI Solver, credits give you access to advanced AI-powered learning features. With the Pro Plan, you receive 50 credits per day, while the Ultra Plan offers unlimited credits with no daily limit. Most questions or solutions use 1 credit, although more complex or multi-step problems may require additional credits. This credit system ensures fair usage and consistent performance for all users.

2\. Will my subscription automatically renew?

Yes. After your first payment, your subscription will automatically renew at the beginning of each billing cycle (monthly or yearly). If the renewal does not go through—for example, due to an expired card or insufficient funds—your account will be downgraded to the Free Plan in the next billing period.

3\. Can I cancel my subscription any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. After canceling, you’ll continue to have access to paid features until the end of your current billing period, and no additional charges will be made after that.

4\. What happens if I run out my free daily credit limit?

If you exceed your daily credits on the Free Plan, you’ll need to wait until the next day for your credits to refresh. If this happens frequently, you may want to upgrade to the Pro Plan, which offers 50 credits per day, or the Ultra Plan for unlimited access to learning and problem-solving features.

5\. Is my payment information secure?

All payments are processed through Stripe, so your credit card details are never stored on our servers. Stripe supports multiple payment methods and delivers a secure, encrypted, and dependable payment experience for all users.
