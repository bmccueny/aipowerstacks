### Frequently Asked Questions

###### How do credits work?

Using image tools consumes credits, with the amount varying by function. The cost for AI image and video generation depends on the selected model and quality. No credits are charged for any failed operations or generations.

###### How to get more credits?

You can upgrade to a higher-credit plan anytime by paying the difference. If the highest plan is insufficient, contact [contact@mockuplabs.ai](mailto:contact@mockuplabs.ai) for custom credit packages. You can also earn free credits by submitting product feedback.

###### Will credits expire?

Credits from monthly plans are only valid within the current billing cycle and expire without rollover. Annual plan credits are issued monthly and also reset at the end of each month without accumulation.

###### Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. After downgrading or canceling, You keep your current benefits until the billing cycle ends. Unused monthly credits will expire upon switch.

###### Can I request a refund?

You may request a refund if you have used fewer than 30 credits and the payment was made within the last 15 days. Please cancel your subscription and contact us at [contact@mockuplabs.ai](mailto:contact@mockuplabs.ai). Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

###### Can I try paid features before subscribing?

Absolutely! Our Free Plan allows you to unlimited use of core features and fully test all paid features: every premium model includes free trials (2 for Basic, 1 for Pro). Try them to decide your best fit.

###### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and Stripe.

###### Can I use it commercially?

Members may use content generated on Mockuplabs for commercial purposes, in accordance with the original model licenses and platform terms of service.
