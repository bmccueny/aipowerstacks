### What is VoooAI?

The biggest difference between VoooAI and ChatGPT/Midjourney:

• Other AI: Say one sentence → Get one result
• VoooAI: Say one sentence → Auto-design workflow → Execute multiple steps → Produce multiple connected results

For example, say "Help me make a coffee product promo video", VoooAI will automatically: generate product image → create video → add background music → add narration.

No dragging nodes, no configuring parameters. [View Details](https://voooai.com/features)

### What is the difference between VoooAI and traditional workflows?

Compared to traditional workflow editors, VoooAI's self-developed NL2Workflow mechanism can generate workflows through natural language, with very few node classes, extremely low learning costs, and can quickly generate text and media materials that meet user intentions and engine prompt standards. Users only need to execute directly or execute after fine-tuning to obtain the final product. The operation is very simple, allowing you to experience the joy of workflow creation that is not exclusively for professionals. [View Details](https://voooai.com/workflow-comparison)

### What are the features of the task queue function?

VoooAI supports efficient task queues, allowing you to perform multiple work generation tasks. You don't have to wait, submit multiple ideas and allow the system to intelligently manage and optimize the execution order of each task. Each task can be customized based on specific parameters or adjusted quickly using saved configurations as template references. It supports real-time progress tracking and prioritization, making your creative process more seamless.

### Are my works and prompts saved on servers?

Absolutely not! VoooAI has a zero data retention policy. Your prompts and generated works are never stored on our servers. Once generation is complete, images are downloaded directly to your device, and we maintain no records on our servers. We take your privacy very seriously.
