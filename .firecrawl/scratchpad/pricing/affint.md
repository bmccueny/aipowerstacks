## Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our FAQs—designed to make your journey with Affint smooth and straightforward

### How do credits work?

Credits are the units we use to measure the work Affint does for you. They're spent based on the number of tokens generated and any tool actions performed. The more complex the task, the more credits you'll use. For example, deep research typically uses about 15 credits, creating a spreadsheet uses around 25, and producing a presentation can take about 55 credits.

### What happens if I use all my credits?

When you've used up your credits, you can either wait for them to reset with your next billing cycle, switch to a higher plan, or buy extra credits if you're on a Plus subscription or higher.

### Do you have discounted rates for yearly subscriptions?

Absolutely, annual plans come with lower rates than monthly ones. We provide 25% OFF on Annual Plans. You'll see your savings when selecting your subscription.

### Will my unused credits carry over to the next month?

No, credits renew with each new billing cycle, and unused credits don't roll over. To get more, just upgrade your plan or purchase additional credits.

### Is it possible to change or cancel my plan anytime?

Absolutely! You can adjust your plan or cancel it anytime through your account settings. The update will take effect in your next billing period.
