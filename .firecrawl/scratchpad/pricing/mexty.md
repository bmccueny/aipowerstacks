## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do credits work?

Credits are used to process AI requests. Each request consumes credits based on the number of tokens processed. You can purchase credits at any time and they never expire.

### What is included in the free trial?

All plans include a 10 free credits trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start.

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and unused credits are preserved.

### What about enterprise features?

Enterprise plans include dedicated support, custom integrations, SSO, and advanced security features. Contact our sales team for details.
