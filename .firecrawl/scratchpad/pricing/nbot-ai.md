## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

What are credits?

Credits power AI features in nbot.ai. Different actions cost different amounts: Chat Q&A costs 1 credit per message, running a tracker update costs 2 credits, and generating a podcast summary costs 10 credits. Every user gets daily credits that reset at midnight UTC.

What happens when I run out of credits?

If you run out of credits, you can wait for your daily credits to reset at midnight UTC, or upgrade to a paid plan to get monthly bonus credits. Your trackers will continue to work, but AI-powered features like Chat and Podcast generation will be limited.

Do unused credits roll over?

Daily credits reset every day at midnight UTC and don't roll over. Monthly bonus credits (for Plus and Pro subscribers) are granted on your billing date and can roll over to the next month, up to 50% of your monthly allocation.

Can I try before subscribing?

Yes! Our Free plan includes 20 daily credits with full feature access. You can create 2 AI Trackers, use Chat, generate podcasts, and experience everything nbot.ai has to offer. This gives you ample opportunity to explore before deciding to upgrade.

What's the difference between tiers?

Higher tiers offer more AI Trackers, more updates per day, faster data refresh rates, and monthly bonus credits. Plus gives you 10 trackers and 1,000 monthly credits. Pro gives you 20 trackers, 2,500 monthly credits, and the freshest data with priority support.

Is there a yearly billing option?

Yes! Save approximately 17% with yearly billing. Plus is $99.99/year (vs $9.99/month), and Pro is $199.99/year (vs $19.99/month). You can switch between monthly and yearly billing anytime from your subscription settings.

How do I manage my subscription?

Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Manage Subscription" from the dropdown menu. From there, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. There are no hidden fees or lock-in contracts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for secure payment processing and accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as various digital wallet options.
