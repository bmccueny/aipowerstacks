## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you offer a free trial?

Most people get started with a 7-day free trial on the Solo or Teams plan to experience Mavis AI risk-free. During your trial, you get 30 free credits and access to all Mavis AI features.



### What's the difference between standard, premium and automated articles?

**• Standard articles** are generated based on a keyword or title and rely on Mavis AI’s general knowledge. _Cost: 1 credit_

**• Premium articles** use URLs as references to ensure accuracy and are ideal for news content, as they pull from real-time sources. _Cost: 2 credits_

**•** **Automated articles** allow you to set up recurring content generation on auto-pilot. You can schedule daily news articles around specific topics or industries without needing to provide new inputs each time. _Cost: 3 credits_



### Can Mavis AI generate news articles automatically?

Yes, Mavis AI specializes in news article automation. You can choose a topic, set the publication frequency (e.g. daily) and the number number of stories to cover. Mavis AI will then generate news articles covering the latest news and developments on that topic every day, and can even publish them to your WordPress or Medium blog automatically.

### What's the difference between the Solo and Team plans?

The Solo plan is for one user and includes 60 credits, while the Teams plan supports up to 5 users and includes 140 credits, making it ideal for teams. Additional credits and seats can be purchased from inside the Mavis AI dashboard.



### Do you offer yearly price plans?

You can pay annually up-front to save 20% compared to the monthly plan.

You can start a 7-day free trial of the Solo or Teams plans to try Mavis AI's features risk-free today before committing to an annual plan.

### Can I purchase additional credits?

Yes, additional credits can be purchased from your account's Usage page.

### What's the cancellation policy?

You can cancel at any time from the billing page and will retain access to your plan until the end of the subscription period. After canceling, you will be switched to a free plan.

If you have purchased additional credits, you can continue to use them to generate content even on a free plan.



### Do unused credits carry over to the next month?

Credits that are part of your Solo or Teams plan do not carry over to the next month. However, additional credits that you purchased will carry over. Purchased credits will remain in your account for 2 years if not used.
