### What is Final Round AI?

Final Round AI is an all-in-one AI interview assistant tool designed for both interview preparation and live performance support. It provides live interview guidance with tools like [AI Interview Copilot](https://www.finalroundai.com/interview-copilot). It can simulate real interview scenarios for [AI mock interview](https://www.finalroundai.com/ai-mock-interview) practice. It provides detailed post-interview feedback, including performance scores, speech clarity insights, engagement analysis, and AI-powered improvement suggestions.

### How to use Final Round AI?

To get started, upload your resume and select your target role to personalize the interview context. You can customize response length, tone, and model preferences for tailored guidance. For live AI interviews, download the desktop app to enable Stealth Mode and launch Interview Copilot when an interview is scheduled. You can also complete onboarding to begin AI mock interviews with customized, role-specific AI simulations designed around your background.

### What does an all-in-one AI interview tool mean?

An all-in-one AI interview assistance tool like Final Round AI provides preparation, live assistance, and post-interview feedback within one tool. Instead of using separate apps for practice, answer generation, and evaluation, candidates can manage the entire interview process in one structured system.

### How does Final Round AI support the full interview process?

Final Round AI supports the full interview lifecycle by offering AI mock interviews for practice, real-time assistance during live interviews, and detailed feedback to improve future performance. This structured flow helps candidates continuously refine their answers and communication.

### What types of interviews does Final Round AI assist with?

Final Round AI provides real-time interview assistance across technical, coding and behavioral interviews, while also supporting system design discussions and case-based interviews. The tool personalizes guidance based on the user's uploaded resume, selected role, and background details, helping generate structured answers during live interview conversations with the interviewer.

### What is an AI interview assistant?

An AI interview assistant is a tool that provides real-time guidance during job interviews. It listens to interview questions and generates structured response suggestions that appear on the candidate's screen while the interview is happening. These prompts are visible only to the candidate and not to the interviewer, allowing the candidate to read the guidance and respond naturally. Tools like Final Round AI also include features such as AI mock interviews and preparation tools so candidates can practice before the actual interview.

### Is using an AI interview assistant legal or ethical?

AI interview assistants are tools designed to help candidates prepare for and navigate interviews more effectively. Company policies regarding external tools can vary, so candidates should review the interview guidelines provided by each employer. Many candidates use AI interview assistants to practice responses, structure answers, and improve communication during interview preparation.
