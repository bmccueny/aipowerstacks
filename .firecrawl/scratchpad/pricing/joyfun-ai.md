## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are Credits and how do they work?

Credits are the currency you use to generate videos on Joyfun. Instead of monthly subscriptions, we offer Credit packs as a **one-time purchase**. This gives you full control over your spending with no recurring charges or hidden fees. You simply buy a pack of Credits and use them as you need.

### How can I get more Credits?

You can purchase additional Credit packs at any time from our pricing page. Our ‘pay-as-you-go’ system allows you to buy more Credits only when you run out or need more for a larger project. We may also offer bonus Credits during special promotions or events!

### What can I use my Credits for?

Your Credits unlock the full power of the Joyfun platform. You can use them for **all of our generative AI tools**, including our popular **AI Image to Video** and **AI Text to Video** features. There are no feature-specific restrictions—one credit balance gives you access to everything, now and in the future.

### Do my Credits expire?

No, your purchased Credits **never expire**. Once you buy them, they are permanently yours to use whenever you wish, whether it’s next week or next year. Your creative potential doesn’t have a deadline.

### What is your refund policy?

We offer a refund under specific conditions to ensure fairness. You are eligible for a full refund if you meet **both** of the following criteria:

- The refund request is made within **48 hours** of the purchase.
- You have used **100 Credits or less** from the purchased pack.

If you meet both of these conditions, please contact our support team at **support@joyfun.ai** with your purchase details to request a refund.

### Can I transfer Credits to another user or redeem them for cash?

No, Credits are non-transferable and cannot be shared with or sent to other users. They also have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for money.
