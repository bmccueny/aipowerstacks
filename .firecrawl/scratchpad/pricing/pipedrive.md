## Frequently asked questions

- What is CRM pricing and how is it determined? What factors affect CRM software cost?






CRM pricing refers to the cost of implementing and using [customer relationship management software](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/products/what-is-crm). The price is determined by factors like number of users, features included in the software, integrations and level of support provided by CRM companies.

- How much does a CRM cost on average?






CRM costs vary widely depending on the CRM company and features included. On average, the typical cost of CRM software ranges from less than a hundred to several hundred dollars per user per year, with some enterprise-level solutions costing even more. To find the best CRM software prices, research and compare CRM providers based on their pricing plans and features. Pipedrive offers different solutions at price points to match users’ requirements. Learn more on our [CRM price comparison](https://www.pipedrive.com/crm-comparison) pages.

- Can my co-workers join my free trial?






Yes, you can invite as many people as you want to join your Pipedrive trial. Simply click on your profile in the top right corner of your screen and select “Add more users” from the drop-down menu.

- Will my data be safe?






Protecting your business data is one of our highest priorities. Learn more about our rigorous [security and privacy](https://www.pipedrive.com/features/privacy-security) standards.

- What is an add-on?






Add-ons are additional Pipedrive tools that you can add to any plan. You are charged for add-ons per company, not per user.

- What’s the difference between annual and monthly billing?






The difference is [how often you pay](https://support.pipedrive.com/en/article/what-is-the-difference-between-annual-and-monthly-billing-in-pipedrive) your subscription fee. With monthly billing, you will be charged on the same day each month. With annual billing, you are charged once for the whole year.

- Which payment methods do you accept?






There are [different ways](https://support.pipedrive.com/en/article/what-are-the-accepted-payment-methods-for-pipedrive) to pay for Pipedrive including major credit cards, debit cards and PayPal. Your payment options depend on the country you live in.

- How can I contact customer support?






Our friendly support team is ready to help out. [Here’s how to get in touch.](https://support.pipedrive.com/en/contact-us)

- What are the prices associated with CRM?






As well as the cost of the customer relationship management software itself, which differs based on pricing tier, some providers also charge users for implementation, setup, training, data migration, support and more. You may also be able to purchase credits to get access to more features or more access to the features already on your plan. Try to find a CRM that’s transparent about its pricing. Pipedrive has no hidden costs, making it an excellent choice for anyone with a fixed budget.

- What does it cost to implement a CRM?






The cost of implementing a CRM varies based on factors such as business size, software choice, user count, customization and integration needs. On-premises setups usually have higher upfront costs. Some CRM providers charge users for implementation. If you choose Pipedrive, implementation is free when you sign up for a pricing plan that’s over $400 a year. Costs differ widely, so a thorough evaluation is essential for an accurate estimate.
